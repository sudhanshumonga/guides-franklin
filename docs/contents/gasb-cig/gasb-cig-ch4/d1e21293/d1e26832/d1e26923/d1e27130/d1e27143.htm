Chapter 4, Question 4.51.1

4.51.1.Q—What was intended in paragraph 73 of Statement 14, as amended, with regard to the elimination of profit on transactions between a participant and a joint venture? Is the transaction itself eliminated, or only the effect of profit on the transaction?
A—The intent was to prevent a participant from reporting net income on "transactions with itself." The participant's share of the net income on transactions with the joint venture would be treated as an additional equity interest in the joint venture rather than earnings from the joint venture. The total change in the equity interest in the joint venture would be unaffected, but the net increase in the equity interest would result from two factors rather than one.
