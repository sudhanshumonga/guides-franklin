Chapter Z, Question Z.54.54

Z.54.54.Q—A city council has adopted a plan to reduce the city’s fund balance over the next five years to a specified level by raising less revenue than is required to cover appropriations. The plan is to reduce fund balance by two million dollars in each of the next three years and by one million in the fourth and fifth years. Should the city classify the entire eight million as assigned fund balance?
A—No. The city would classify as assigned, each year, an amount not greater than the projected excess of expenditures over expected revenues for that year. (See also Question Z.54.14.)
