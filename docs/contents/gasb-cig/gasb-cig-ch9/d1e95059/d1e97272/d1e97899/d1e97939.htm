Chapter 9, Question 9.30.2

9.30.2.Q—Statement 44 requires that the full calculation of the legal debt margin be presented for the most recent year. May a government present the calculation for all 10 years of the schedule?
A—Yes, as long as doing so enhances the schedule's understandability or its usefulness and the schedule's focus remains on the key debt limit information—the limit, net debt applicable to the limit, the debt margin, and the ratio. (See Exhibit B-11 in nonauthoritative Appendix B9-2.)
