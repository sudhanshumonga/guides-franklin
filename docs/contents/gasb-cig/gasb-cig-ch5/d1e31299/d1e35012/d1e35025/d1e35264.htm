Chapter 5, Question 5.116.8

5.116.8.Q—Does Statement 68, as amended, apply to a governmental employer that provides pensions through a single-employer plan that is administered by the employees’ union if benefits are negotiated periodically (for example, every three to five years)?
A—If the pension plan is administered through a trust (or equivalent arrangement) that meets the criteria of paragraph 4 of Statement 68, Statement 68, as amended, is applicable to the state or local government whose employees are provided with pensions through the plan. This is the case regardless of the nature of the entity administering the plan or whether the benefits provided through the plan are subject to periodic negotiation.
