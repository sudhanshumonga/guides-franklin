Chapter Z, Question Z.54.17

Z.54.17.Q—Is the answer to Question Z.54.16 different if the capital projects fund also includes resources that are committed and assigned to other purposes?
A—Yes. The negative amount should first reduce assigned fund balances in that fund until they are completely eliminated. The amount of the negative balance that exceeds assigned fund balances should be reported as a negative unassigned fund balance. Committed fund balances should not be reduced by overexpenditure for other purposes in that fund.
