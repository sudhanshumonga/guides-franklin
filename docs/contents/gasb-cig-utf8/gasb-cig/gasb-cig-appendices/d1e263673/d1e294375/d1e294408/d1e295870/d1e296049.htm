Note 6:Disclosure of segment information. This disclosure is required by paragraph 122. The segments in this illustrative note represent two departments (that meet the criteria as segments) reported in a single fund. If a segment is reported as a major fund, the required information is already presented; therefore, the disclosures illustrated here would not be required.
