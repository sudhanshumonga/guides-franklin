Decline a connection request

  1. On the menu bar, click Org Connect.
  2. Perform one of the following actions:

    Task

    Steps

    Accept an invitation from the All organizations page.

    1. Click All organizations.
    2. In the Connected apps column, click the Pending acceptance icon. The connection details are displayed.
    3. Read the connection agreement and click Decline.

    Accept an invitation from the My network page.

    1. Click My network.
    2. In the Connected apps column, click the Pending acceptance icon.
    3. Read the connection agreement and click Decline.

    Accept an invitation from the Mailbox.

    1. Click Mailbox.
    2. In the Received message list, click a Connection request -  message. The connection request message opens.
    3. Read the message and connection agreement. Click Decline.
