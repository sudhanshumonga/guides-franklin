Chapter 7, Question 7.22.1

7.22.1.Q—Can the statement of net position be presented in a classified format, similar to what is required for enterprise funds?
A—Yes. Governments are encouraged to present assets and liabilities in order of their relative liquidity but may, instead, use the classified format, which distinguishes between all current and long-term assets and liabilities. If the classified format is used, a government is not required to distinguish between current and long-term deferred outflows of resources and deferred inflows of resources. Exhibit 1 in nonauthoritative Appendix B7-2 illustrates a classified format.
