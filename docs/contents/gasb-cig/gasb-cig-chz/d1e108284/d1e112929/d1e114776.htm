Chapter Z, Question Z.54.59

Z.54.59.Q—A county government maintains a special revenue fund to account for restricted grant proceeds. At year end there is $45,000 cash in the fund, but the county has issued purchase orders encumbering $50,000, based on the expectation of receiving grant revenues that are not yet recognized as receivable. How should the county classify the fund balance in that special revenue fund?
A—The county should report restricted fund balance of $45,000 (rather than $50,000 as restricted and $5,000 as a deficit in unassigned). The amount reported as restricted fund balance should not exceed the restricted assets in a fund.
