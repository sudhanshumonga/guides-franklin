7.9.1.Q—What are land improvements?
A—Land improvements consist of betterments, other than buildings, that ready land for its intended use. Examples of land improvements include site improvements such as excavation, fill, grading, and utility installation; removal, relocation, or reconstruction of property of others, such as railroads and telephone and power lines; retaining walls; parking lots; fencing; and landscaping.
