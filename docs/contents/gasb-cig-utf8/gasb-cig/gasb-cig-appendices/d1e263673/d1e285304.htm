Appendix B4-2: Statement 14, as Amended—Illustrative Examples
This nonauthoritative appendix provides examples to illustrate how Statement 14, as amended, would be applied to particular sets of hypothetical circumstances. Case 13a illustrates certain provisions of Statement 61. Cases 20–28 illustrate how the criteria in paragraph 5 of Statement 39, would be applied. That paragraph amends Statement 14 by providing specific criteria to consider when a potential component unit is not fiscally dependent on a primary government and the primary government does not appoint a voting majority of the potential component unit's governing board.
Note: In arriving at conclusions for these cases, all financial benefit and burden relationships were considered material from the standpoint of the primary government or the potential component unit, unless otherwise noted.
