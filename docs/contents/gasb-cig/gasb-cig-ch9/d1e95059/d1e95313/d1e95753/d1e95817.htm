Chapter 9, Question 9.11.3

9.11.3.Q—May a government present information about debt service ratios in a separate schedule, rather than combining it with the information about changes in governmental fund balances?
A—Yes. Statement 44 eliminated a previous requirement to present a separate schedule because it repeated information already presented in other schedules. However, as long as a government meets the requirements of the Statement—specifically, that debt service be shown in its principal and interest components, and that the ratio be calculated as (total debt service ÷ total noncapital expenditures)—it does not matter whether the information appears in the same or a separate schedule. If a government presents a separate schedule, it would be helpful to include a note in the changes in fund balances schedule directing the reader to the debt service ratio information. (For example, see the note in Exhibit I-7 in nonauthoritative Appendix B9-2, which directs the reader to the schedule in Exhibit I-15 of that Appendix.)
