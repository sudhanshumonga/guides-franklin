B. Individual Component Unit Disclosures
Blended Component Unit. The Sample City Parking Facilities Authority owns and operates the municipal parking facilities in the city. Although it is legally separate from the city, the authority is reported as an enterprise fund as if it were part of the primary government because the city council also serves as the authority's governing board and there is a specific financial benefit or burden relationship between the primary government and the component unit.
Discretely Presented Component Units. The component units columns and rows in the government-wide statement of net position and statement of activities include the financial data of the city's other component units. They are reported in a separate column or a separate row to emphasize that they are legally separate from the city. The Sample City Landfill's five-member governing board is appointed by the city council. The Sample City School District's board is elected by the voters of Sample City.
1.The Sample City Landfill operates the city's solid waste landfill site. The landfill's operating budget is subject to the approval of the city council. The council is also required to approve proposed capital improvements or additions to the landfill's facilities. During the year, the city paid the landfill $1,665,291 for solid waste disposal.
2.The Sample City School District operates the six elementary and two high schools in the city. The school district may not issue debt without the city council's approval. In addition, the district's property tax funding request is also subject to city council approval. The district's taxes are levied under the taxing authority of the city and are included as part of the city's total tax levy. During the year, the city levied and collected $21,893,273 in property taxes for the district and remitted those taxes to the district.
