Invite an unlisted organization

You can connect with any organization. If the organization does not have a  account or has not registered for , they receive an email with instructions to create an account and register when you send your invitation. When the organization creates the account and registers, you are notified that they are available to connect. You can also use the Invite unlisted organization option to send a connection request to an organization that is registered but has set their visibility to not visible.

  1. In the menu bar, click Org Connect > All organizations.
  2. Click Invite unlisted organization.
  3. On the Invite unlisted organization page, in the the Organization name field, type the name of the organization that you want to connect with.
  4. In the Organization name field, type the name of the organization that you want to send the invitation to.
  5. In the Contact name field, type the name of the contact that you want to send the invitation to.
  6. In the Email address field, type the contact's email address.
  7. In the Message field, type a message for the contact.
  8. Click Send invitation.
After you send the invitation, the  owner in the invited organization receives a notification in the  mailbox if they are already registered and by email.
