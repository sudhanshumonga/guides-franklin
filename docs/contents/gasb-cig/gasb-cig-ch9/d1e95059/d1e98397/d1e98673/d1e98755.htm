Chapter 9, Question 9.38.3

9.38.3.Q—How many years should be covered by the schedules presented by pension and other postemployment benefit plans?
A—Unless otherwise specified in the Statement, all schedules should cover the most recent 10 years. If a plan previously presented information for fewer than 10 years, it is encouraged, but not required to retroactively report the full 10 years when it implements Statement 44.
