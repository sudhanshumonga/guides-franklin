Chapter 2, Question 2.20.2

2.20.2.Q—Should royalty income be evaluated in the same manner as rent income for statement of cash flows classification?
A—Yes. Royalty income is similar to rent income for cash flow reporting purposes. (See Question 2.20.1.) The asset underlying the royalties should be evaluated to determine whether it should be treated as an investment for cash flow reporting purposes. If it should, the royalty income should be classified as an investing activity. If not, the royalty income should be classified in the residual category—operating activities.
