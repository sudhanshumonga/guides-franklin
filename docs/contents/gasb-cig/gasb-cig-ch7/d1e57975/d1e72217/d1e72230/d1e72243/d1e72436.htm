Chapter 7, Question 7.91.7

7.91.7.Q—Can a government present some of its required budgetary comparison information (for the general fund, for example) in the basic statements (as provided for in footnote 53 to paragraph 130 of Statement 34) and the remainder in schedules as RSI?
A—No. The required budgetary comparison information—for the general fund and each major special revenue fund that has a legally adopted budget—should be reported together.
