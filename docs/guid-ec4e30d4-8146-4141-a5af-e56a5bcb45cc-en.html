Remove the connection for an app

  1. On the menu bar, click Org Connect > My network.
  2. On the My network page, scroll to or search for the the organization that you want to remove the connection with.
  3. In the Connected apps column, click the icon for the app that you want to remove the connection from (for example, the  icon).
    The Connection details page opens.
  4. On the Connection details page, click Remove below the app icon.
  5. Optionally, in the confirmation dialog box, add a message.
  6. Click Remove.
When the connection is removed an email is sent to the administrators in your organization and the connected organization. Users in the organizations can no longer connect with each other using the app.
