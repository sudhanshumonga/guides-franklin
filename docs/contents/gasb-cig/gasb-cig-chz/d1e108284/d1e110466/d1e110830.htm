Chapter Z, Question Z.48.8

Z.48.8.Q—Paragraph 11 of Statement 48, as amended, states that "proceeds received by the pledging government should be reported as a liability in its statement of net position." Does that requirement change how discounts and premiums are reported?
A—No. Debt issued at a premium or discount should continue to be recognized at its face amount with the discount or premium shown separately as a direct deduction from or addition to the amount of the debt. The provision in paragraph 11 is intended to address situations in which the pledging government receives proceeds but does not issue a debt instrument.
