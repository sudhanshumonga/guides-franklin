Chapter 7, Question 7.23.1

7.23.1.Q—In which component of net position should capital assets with externally imposed restrictions (for example, federal surplus property) be reported?
A—All capital assets, including those that are subject to restrictions, should be included in the net investment in capital assets component of net position. The requirement to segregate restricted and unrestricted components of net position was established primarily to provide information about the availability of financial resources.
