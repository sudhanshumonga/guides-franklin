Chapter Z, Question Z.49.2

Z.49.2.Q—Paragraph 4c of Statement 18 states that postclosure care may include "remedying or containing environmental hazards." When should pollution caused by a landfill subject to Statement 18 be accounted for under the requirements of Statement 49?
A—Determining which landfill pollution events are covered by Statement 49 involves professional judgment, giving consideration to the significance and nature of the remediation work. Paragraph 4a of Statement 49 states that Statement 49 does not apply to obligations within the scope of Statement 18. Statement 18 requires accrual of the estimated total current cost of closure and postclosure care. Minor pollution events that, although unexpected, are not unusual normally would be included in estimates of closure and postclosure care reported under Statement 18. If the remedy involves a large and unusual pollution event, it normally would be accounted for under Statement 49.
