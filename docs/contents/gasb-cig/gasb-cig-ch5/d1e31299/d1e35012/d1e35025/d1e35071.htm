Chapter 5, Question 5.116.2

5.116.2.Q—If the single or agent employer in Question 5.116.1 has a special funding situation, does Statement 68, as amended, apply to the employer?
A—Yes. Regardless of whether the single or agent employer in Question 5.116.1 has a special funding situation, if the employer provides pensions through a defined benefit pension plan that is administered through a trust (or equivalent arrangement) that has the characteristics identified in paragraph 4 of Statement 68, Statement 68, as amended, applies. A single or agent employer that has a special funding situation should follow the provisions of paragraphs 18, 19, and 83–91 of Statement 68, as amended, for pension liabilities to employees.
