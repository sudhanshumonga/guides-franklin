Chapter 7, Question 7.24.16

7.24.16.Q—Paragraph 8 of Statement 63 requires that restricted net position be reported by major categories of restrictions. Furthermore, paragraph 6 of Statement 46, as amended, states that the amount of a primary government’s net position restricted by enabling legislation should be disclosed in the notes to the financial statements. Should the net position restricted by enabling legislation be disclosed using the major categories displayed in the government-wide statement of net position?
A—A government may choose to disclose net position restricted by enabling legislation by major categories of restrictions. Likewise, a government may elect to disclose net position restricted by enabling legislation separately for governmental and business-type activities. However, it is acceptable to disclose a single amount for the primary government. If the government does not have net position restricted by enabling legislation, no disclosure is required.
