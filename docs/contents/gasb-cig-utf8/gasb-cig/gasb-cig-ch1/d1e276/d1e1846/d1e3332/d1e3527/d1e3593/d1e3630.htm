Chapter 1, Question 1.30.2

1.30.2.Q—What sources are available to determine FDIC coverage?
A—To obtain a general understanding of FDIC coverage, including FDIC responses to selected technical inquiries, refer to the FDIC website at www.fdic.gov. For specific information about coverage of public funds, consult the Federal Deposit Insurance Act (12 United States Code [U.S.C.] 1811 et seq.) and the FDIC's deposit insurance regulations (12 Code of Federal Regulations [C.F.R.] Part 330).
