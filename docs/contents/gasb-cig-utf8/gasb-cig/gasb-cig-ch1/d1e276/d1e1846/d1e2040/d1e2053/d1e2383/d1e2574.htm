Chapter 1, Question 1.16.7

1.16.7.Q—Is a deposit exposed to custodial credit risk if collateral is pledged by one affiliate of a bank holding company and it is held in the trust department of another affiliate bank in the government's name?
A—No. The custodial trust department would be viewed as the counterparty's trust department and the deposit would be held in the government's name. No custodial credit risk disclosures would be required.
