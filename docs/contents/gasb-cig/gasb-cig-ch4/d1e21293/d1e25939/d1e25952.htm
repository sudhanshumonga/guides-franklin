Chapter 4, Question 4.36.1

4.36.1.Q—Can budgetary data for a discretely presented component unit be presented in the reporting entity's budget-to-actual comparison as RSI or in the basic financial statements?
A—No. Paragraph 130 of Statement 34, as amended, clearly limits the comparison to the primary government's general fund and major special revenue funds with legally adopted annual budgets. Including data for discretely presented component units in the budgetary comparison schedule would be in conflict with the limitations of that requirement. Budgetary comparisons for discretely presented component units, however, can be presented as supplementary information.
