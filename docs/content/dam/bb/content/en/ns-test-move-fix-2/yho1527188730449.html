Accept a connection request

When you receive a connection request,  is displayed beside the app icon on the All organizations and My network pages.
  1. On the menu bar, click Org Connect.
  2. Perform one of the following actions:

    Task

    Steps

    Accept an invitation from the All organizations page.

    1. Click All organizations.
    2. In the Connected apps column, click . The connection details are displayed.
    3. Read the connection agreement and click Accept.

    Accept an invitation from the My network page.

    1. Click My network.
    2. In the Connected apps column, click . The connection details are displayed.
    3. Read the connection agreement and click Accept.

    Accept an invitation from the Mailbox.

    1. Click Mailbox.
    2. In the Received message list, click a Connection request -  message. The connection request message opens.
    3. Read the connection agreement. Click Accept. 
After you accept a connection request, the  owner in the invited organization receives a notification in the  mailbox and by email.  users can begin to search for and connect with contacts in each organization.
