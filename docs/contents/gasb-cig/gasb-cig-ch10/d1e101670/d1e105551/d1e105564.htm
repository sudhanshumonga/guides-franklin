Chapter 10, Question 10.46.1

10.46.1.Q—A public university’s endowment fund indirectly holds derivative instruments as part of the investment assets reported by an external investment pool. Should the public university "look through" the investment pool to these derivative instruments and make the required derivative instrument disclosures?
A—No. A position in an external investment pool is in itself an investment type (paragraph 4 of Statement 40).
