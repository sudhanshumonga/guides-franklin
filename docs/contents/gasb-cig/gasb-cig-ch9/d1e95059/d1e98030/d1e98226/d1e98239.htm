See also Question 5.112.1 for additional discussion about the presentation of statistical section information about principal employers.
9.34.1.Q—How many employers should a government include in the schedule of principal employers?
A—The basic requirement of paragraph 34 of Statement 44 is to present the 10 largest employers in a government's geographic area. However, if fewer than 10 employers account for 50 percent or more of total employment, a government may present fewer than 10 employers. A government also may include more than 10 employers if it believes doing so would be meaningful and would help to meet the objectives set forth in paragraph 6 of that Statement.
