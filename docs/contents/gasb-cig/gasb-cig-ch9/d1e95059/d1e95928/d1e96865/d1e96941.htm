Chapter 9, Question 9.20.3

9.20.3.Q—A government reporting revenue capacity information about a particular tax (a) has a component unit that also levies the same type of tax or (b) participates in a joint venture that levies the same type of tax. Should the tax rates of the government's component unit or joint venture be shown as direct rates or overlapping rates?
A—If the government has the ability to set, modify, or approve the revenue rates of the component unit or joint venture, the rates should be reported by the government as direct rates. All other rates set by the component unit or joint venture would be considered overlapping rates.
