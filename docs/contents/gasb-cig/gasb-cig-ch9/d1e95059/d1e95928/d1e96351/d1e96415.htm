Chapter 9, Question 9.15.3

9.15.3.Q—The total direct rate is presented in the schedule of revenue rates. Does it have to be presented in the revenue base schedule as well?
A—Yes. The total direct rate is required to be presented in both schedules to help the user crosswalk them. This can be especially important in cases where the period in which taxes are levied or are payable differs from the period for which the taxes were levied. (See, for example, the scenario described in Question 9.23.5.)
