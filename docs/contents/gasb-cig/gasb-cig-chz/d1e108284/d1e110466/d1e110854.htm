Chapter Z, Question Z.48.9

Z.48.9.Q—A water and sewer enterprise fund sells bonds to provide financing to construct a revenue producing facility and secures the debt with a pledge of the revenues that will be generated by that facility. Is the pledge of those future revenues subject to the requirements of Statement 48?
A—Yes. Even though footnote 1 of Statement 48 provides that "future revenues do not include potential revenues from a source not in existence at the time of the transaction," the focus of that exclusion is on transactions involving revenues that will be generated by a source to be created by the transferee, not the pledging government.
