Chapter 7, Question 7.85.4

7.85.4.Q—Paragraph 115e of Statement 34 requires disclosure of a government’s capitalization policy. What information should be included?
A—There are no specific requirements for capitalization policies. However, capitalization policies may include (a) capitalization thresholds, (b) the methods used for determining historical cost or acquisition value, and (c) the extent of infrastructure capitalization, including (for phase 1 and phase 2 governments) whether infrastructure acquired prior to June 30, 1980, is reported and (for phase 3 governments) whether infrastructure acquired prior to implementation of Statement 34 is reported.
