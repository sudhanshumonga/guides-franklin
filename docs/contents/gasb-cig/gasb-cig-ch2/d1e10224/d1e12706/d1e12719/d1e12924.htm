Chapter 2, Question 2.29.6

2.29.6.Q—Under the direct method for reporting cash flows from operating activities, are governments required to separately report transactions with discretely presented component units?
A—No. Paragraph 31 of Statement 9, as amended, requires separate reporting of cash flows from interfund services provided and used but does not require a similar distinction for transactions with component units. Governments may provide those additional details but are not required to do so. However, paragraph 128 of Statement 34 requires disclosure of the nature and amount of significant transactions between the primary government and each major component unit.
