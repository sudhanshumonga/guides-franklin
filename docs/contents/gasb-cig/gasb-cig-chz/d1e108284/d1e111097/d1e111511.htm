Chapter Z, Question Z.49.8

Z.49.8.Q—A state operates an insurance program that indemnifies local governments for pollution remediation obligations. Should expected payments from the insurance program reduce the measurement of a local government's pollution remediation expense?
A—Yes. As provided in paragraph 19 of Statement 49, "expected insurance recoveries from policies that indemnify the government for its pollution remediation obligations" should reduce the measurement of the expense.
