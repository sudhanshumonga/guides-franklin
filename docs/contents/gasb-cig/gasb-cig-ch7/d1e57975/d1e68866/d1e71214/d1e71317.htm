Chapter 7, Question 7.77.4

7.77.4.Q—Paragraph 106 of Statement 34 does not require a separate column for component units that are fiduciary in nature. How should those component units be displayed in the fiduciary fund financial statements?
A—Each fiduciary component unit should be reported within the appropriate fiduciary fund types, rather than aggregated in a separate fiduciary component units column.
