App permissions

Certain permissions are required for  to function correctly. When you download the app or access certain features, you are prompted to allow access to these permissions. The following table lists the required permissions, and how  uses each permission.

Permission

Use

Calendar
  • Show your calendar agenda and invitations

Contacts

  • Display your contacts in emails, text messages, and call logs

Phone

  • Display your call logs
Storage
  • Attach content from your memory card to emails

Change your permission settings

You can deny or allow each permission for an app from your device settings. However, denying permissions may restrict your access to certain features within the app.
