Note 2:Information about long-term liabilities. This disclosure is required by paragraph 119. It presents the beginning and ending balances and increases and decreases for the year for each major type of long-term liability. In addition, paragraphs 119c and d require disclosure of the portion of each item that is due within one year, and which governmental funds have liquidated the long-term operating liabilities in the past. Other presentation techniques may be used.
