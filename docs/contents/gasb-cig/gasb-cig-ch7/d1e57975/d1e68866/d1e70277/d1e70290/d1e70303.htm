Chapter 7, Question 7.69.1

7.69.1.Q—Are combining statements for internal service funds required in the basic financial statements?
A—No. Combining statements for internal service funds are accorded the same status as combining statements for nonmajor funds—they should not be included in the basic financial statements, but may be presented as supplementary information. (See Question 7.48.2 about combining statements in a CAFR.)
