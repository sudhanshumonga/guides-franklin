Chapter 7, Question 7.91.6

7.91.6.Q—A government presents its budgetary comparison in a schedule as RSI. Can budget-to-actual comparisons for capital projects and debt service funds be included in that schedule?
A—No. Paragraph 130 of Statement 34, as amended, clearly limits the comparison to special revenue funds with legally adopted annual budgets—specifically those that are deemed to be "major." (See Question 7.91.5.) Including capital projects funds and debt service funds in the budgetary comparison schedule would be in conflict with the limitations of that requirement.
