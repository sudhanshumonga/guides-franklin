Appendix B7-3: Statement 34, as Amended—Exercises
This appendix presents a variety of "how-to" exercises to help with the implementation of certain requirements of Statement 34, as amended. The guidance provided here is illustrative only and is not authoritative. The purpose of these exercises is to demonstrate practical ways in which preparers can apply specific provisions of the standard. These are only suggested approaches-preparers may discover that other methods work better in their particular situations. The exercises are intended to be as comprehensive as possible and, therefore, may include issues and complications that many governments will not need to face on a regular basis.
