Chapter 9, Question 9.10.1

9.10.1.Q—Should restricted, committed, and assigned fund balances be presented by purpose, as required by Statement 54 for the governmental funds balance sheet?
A—Paragraph 12a of Statement 44, as amended, requires presentation of the required classifications of fund balance. Governments may present restricted, committed, and assigned fund balances by purpose in the statistical section to the extent they believe it would be meaningful to the users of their financial reports and would help to achieve the objectives of financial trends information.
