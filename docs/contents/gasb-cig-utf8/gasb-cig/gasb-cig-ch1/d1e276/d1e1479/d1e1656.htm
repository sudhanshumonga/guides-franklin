Chapter 1, Question 1.9.7

1.9.7.Q—Is a debt security issued by a federal government-sponsored enterprise (GSE) that has only the implicit guarantee of the federal government and is held by a state or local government as an investment subject to credit risk disclosures?
A—Yes. A credit risk disclosure is required if a GSE only has the federal government’s implicit guarantee (paragraph 7 of Statement 40, as amended). However, as the structure of financial markets change, whether a GSE has an implicit or an explicit guarantee may change. A credit risk disclosure is based on the status of a federal guarantee as of the date of the financial statements.
