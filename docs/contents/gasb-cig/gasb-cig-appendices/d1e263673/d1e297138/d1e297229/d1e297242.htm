Exhibit 2 is a modification of Exhibit 2 in nonauthoritative Appendix B7-1. It illustrates the use of an additional column in the statement of activities to display the allocation of indirect expenses (from the general government and interest on long-term debt functions) to the various functions and programs. Indirect expenses are presented in a separate column to enhance comparability (of direct expenses by function) between governments that allocate indirect expenses and those that do not.
