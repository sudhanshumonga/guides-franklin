Chapter Z, Question Z.33.25

Z.33.25Q—The federal government reimburses state and local governments for part of the interest expense on their taxable bonds issued under certain bond programs, such as Build America Bonds and Recovery Zone Economic Development Bonds. Should the reimbursement of interest costs be reported as a reduction of bond interest expense?
A—No. The federal reimbursement is a separate nonexchange transaction and should be recognized as nonexchange revenue when all eligibility requirements are met. Thus, the payment of interest on the qualifying bonds is reported gross, not netted with the federal reimbursement.
