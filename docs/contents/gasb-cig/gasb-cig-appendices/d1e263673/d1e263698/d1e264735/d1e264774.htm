The system's exposure to foreign currency risk is as follows:
The system's investments in foreign-currency-denominated corporate bonds were rated AAA by Standard & Poor's and Fitch Ratings, and Aaa by Moody's Investors Service, consistent with its credit risk policy. The system's investment policy permits it to invest up to 9.0 percent of total investments in foreign-currency-denominated investments. The system's current position is 6.5 percent.
Not presented in this illustration, among other investment disclosures, is the pension system's schedule of investments by type and by credit risk disclosures. If the system has entered into derivative instruments to manage its risks, additional disclosures may be required.
