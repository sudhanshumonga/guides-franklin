Chapter 4, Question 4.21.3

4.21.3.Q—Are "exchange-like" transactions also not considered manifestations of a financial benefit or burden relationship? Paragraph 27 of Statement 14 discusses only exchange transactions.
A—Yes. As explained in footnote 1 of Statement No. 33, Accounting and Financial Reporting for Nonexchange Transactions, the exchange characteristics of an exchange-like transaction are equivalent to an exchange transaction for purposes of applying paragraph 27 of Statement 14.
