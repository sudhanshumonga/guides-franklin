Chapter 7, Question 7.105.5

7.105.5.Q—Paragraph 152 of Statement 34 states that a government can apply the modified approach as long as one complete condition assessment is available. Does this provision apply to a government that adopts the modified approach subsequent to the date when infrastructure was required to be retroactively reported?
A—Yes. The provisions of paragraph 152 apply to any transition to the modified approach, whether the modified approach is adopted when infrastructure is retroactively reported or at a subsequent date.
