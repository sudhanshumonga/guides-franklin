Chapter Z, Question Z.65.2

Z.65.2.Q—Paragraph 15 of Statement 65 requires the portion of debt issuance costs related to prepaid insurance to be reported as an asset and recognized as an expense over the duration of the related debt. Should the expense be reported as insurance expense or as a component of interest cost?
A—The prepaid insurance component of debt issuance costs should be reported as interest expense. Debt insurance coverage generally is obtained to secure a lower interest rate; therefore, the recognition of the periodic charge from the prepaid amount should be a factor in determining the effective interest cost.
