Appendix B: Nonauthoritative Illustrations
NONAUTHORITATIVE ILLUSTRATIONS
B1.This appendix presents nonauthoritative illustrations related to material in paragraphs 4–17 of this Implementation Guide. The illustrations have not been revised for the effects of Statement 72 or Statement 73.
The facts assumed in these examples are illustrative only and may not be indicative of current economic conditions. These example are not intended to modify or limit the requirements being illustrated or to indicate the Board’s endorsement of the policies or practices shown. Application of certain provisions may require accounting treatments, disclosures, or formats other than those illustrated. In some cases, amounts that may be considered immaterial are used to illustrate specific requirements or alternatives. No inferences regarding determining materiality should be drawn from these illustrations. Amounts presented may include rounding differences.
