Chapter 2, Question 2.7.1

2.7.1.Q—In the private sector, certain investment companies are exempted from presenting a statement of cash flows. Are public-sector investment pools similarly exempt?
A—Yes. Paragraph 17 of Statement 31, as amended, states that "...annual financial reports for governmental external investment pools should include a statement of net position and a statement of changes in net position.... A statement of cash flows is not required to be presented." Paragraph 18 of that Statement, as amended, requires sponsoring governments to use investment trust funds to report the external portions of their investment pools. It states that "... the sponsoring government should present for each investment trust fund a statement of fiduciary net position and a statement of changes in fiduciary net position."
