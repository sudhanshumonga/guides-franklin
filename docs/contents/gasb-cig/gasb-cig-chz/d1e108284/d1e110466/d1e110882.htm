Chapter Z, Question Z.48.10

Z.48.10.Q—Footnote 5 of Statement 48 exempts certain legally separate entities that report as stand-alone BTAs from the pledged revenue disclosure requirements in paragraph 21. Does that exemption apply to separately issued statements of component units?
A—No. The exemption provided for in footnote 5 applies to stand-alone governments. Paragraph 66 of Statement 14 states that stand-alone governments do not meet the definition of a component unit. Consequently, the disclosure requirements in paragraph 21 apply to the separately issued financial statements of component units.
