Chapter 9, Question 9.37.3

9.37.3.Q—Are there particular operating or capital asset indicators that a government should present, such as number of arrests for the police function or lane-miles of roads for the transportation or public works function?
A—Although there are some indicators, such as the number of arrests for the police function or lane-miles of roads for the transportation or public works function, that are commonly used by governments and presented in their statistical sections, Statement 44 does not specify what indicators should be reported. That decision is a matter of professional judgment.
