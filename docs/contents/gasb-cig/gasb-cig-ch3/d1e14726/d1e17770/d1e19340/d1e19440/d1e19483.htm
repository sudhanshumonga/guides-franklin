Chapter 3, Question 3.76.2

3.76.2.Q—A component unit handles its risk financing separately from the rest of the financial reporting entity. What disclosures should it make in its separately issued financial statements?
A—Component units should apply all of the provisions of paragraphs 77–80 of Statement 10, as amended, and paragraph 10 of Statement 30 to their separately issued financial statements as if they were primary governments.
