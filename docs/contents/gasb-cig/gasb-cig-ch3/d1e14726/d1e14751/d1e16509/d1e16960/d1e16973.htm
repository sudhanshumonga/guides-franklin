Chapter 3, Question 3.35.1

3.35.1.Q—What information is required to be presented in required supplementary information (RSI) in separately issued public entity risk pool reports?
A—As discussed in paragraph 7 of Statement 30, RSI consists of two tables:
a.A 10-year revenue and claims development table
b.A two-year reconciliation of total claims liabilities by type of contract.
The 10-year revenue and claims development table shows trends in current claims and development in prior years' claims. It also shows, over time, how well a pool is performing its underwriting function and its claims estimation (loss reserving) function.
The two-year reconciliation of total claims liabilities by type of contract provides additional information about the pool's operations. For example, disaggregated claim liability information shows the extent to which changes in liabilities for claims costs and related expenses are due to current claims versus developments in prior years' claims.
