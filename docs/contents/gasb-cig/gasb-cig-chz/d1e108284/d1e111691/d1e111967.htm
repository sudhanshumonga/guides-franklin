Chapter Z, Question Z.51.3

Z.51.3.Q—In a sale of future revenues executed by governments that are not within the same financial reporting entity, does the cost of acquisition of future revenues recorded by the transferee government meet the description of an intangible asset in Statement 51?
A—No. Such a transaction is in a monetary form. Therefore, it does not meet the "nonfinancial nature" characteristic of an intangible asset as described in paragraph 2b of Statement 51.
