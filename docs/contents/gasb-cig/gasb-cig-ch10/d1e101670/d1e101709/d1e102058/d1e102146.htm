Chapter 10, Question 10.6.3

10.6.3.Q—What constitutes a market mechanism that facilitates net settlement under paragraph 13b of Statement 53?
A—A market mechanism may be an institutional arrangement or other agreement, such as a futures exchange. That mechanism enables either party to be relieved of all rights and obligations associated with the derivative instrument and to liquidate its net position without incurring a significant transaction cost, conducting a significant negotiation, and engaging in due diligence, within a time frame that is customary for settlement of the type of contract.
