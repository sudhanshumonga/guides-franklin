Chapter Z, Question Z.48.11

Z.48.11.Q—A state college has a blended component unit that has bonds outstanding, the proceeds of which financed a facility rented by the college. The bonds are secured by rent payments from the college. In the separately issued financial statements of the component unit, are the pledged revenue disclosures in paragraph 21 of Statement 48 required?
A—Yes. The rent constitutes a specific future revenue of the component unit as stipulated in paragraphs 3 and 21 of Statement 48.
