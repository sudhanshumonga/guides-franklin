Chapter 10, Question 10.46.8

10.46.8.Q—Do the disclosure requirements of Statement 40, as amended, and Statement 72 apply to the investments underlying SGICs?
A—Yes. Investments underlying SGICs are an element of the investments reported by the government, belonging to the government and participants. Accordingly, the risk-disclosure requirements of Statement 40, as amended, and the investment-disclosure requirements of Statement 72 should be applied.
