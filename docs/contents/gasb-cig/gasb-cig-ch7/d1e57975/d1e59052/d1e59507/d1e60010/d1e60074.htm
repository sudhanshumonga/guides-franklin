Chapter 7, Question 7.11.3

7.11.3.Q—Are assets not being depreciated—such as land, certain land improvements (see Question 7.13.4), construction in progress, and infrastructure assets reported using the modified approach (see Question 7.16.1)—required to be displayed separately from depreciable assets in the statement of net position?
A—Yes, if they are significant. Capital assets not being depreciated should be reported separately from capital assets being depreciated.
