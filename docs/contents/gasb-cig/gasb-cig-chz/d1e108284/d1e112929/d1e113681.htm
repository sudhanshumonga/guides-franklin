Chapter Z, Question Z.54.15

Z.54.15.Q—Paragraph 12 of Statement 54 requires that the formal action to commit an amount to a specific purpose should occur prior to the end of the reporting period. Does a similar provision apply to the action taken to assign fund balance amounts?
A—No. The determination of the purposes, as well as the amounts for assigned fund balances can be made after the end of the reporting period.
