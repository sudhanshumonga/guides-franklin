Chapter 7, Question 7.47.8

7.47.8.Q—In eliminating the effects of internal service fund activity, what happens to transactions with outside parties?
A—The internal service fund activity pertaining to outside parties is not eliminated, but instead would be included with the appropriate function in the statement of activities. Exercise 4 in nonauthoritative Appendix B7-3 includes an internal service fund that conducts business with outside parties.
