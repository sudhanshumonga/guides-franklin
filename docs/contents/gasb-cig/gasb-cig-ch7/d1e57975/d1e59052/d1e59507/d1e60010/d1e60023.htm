Chapter 7, Question 7.11.1

7.11.1.Q—Should construction in progress be included in capital assets?
A—Yes. Construction in progress should be included with capital assets in the statement of net position. It should be reported with other assets not being depreciated, such as land, certain land improvements, and infrastructure accounted for using the modified approach. (See Question 7.11.3 about presentation of capital assets on the statement of net position.)
