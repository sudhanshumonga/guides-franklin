Chapter 7, Question 7.2.1

7.2.1.Q—Which governmental units should apply the standards of Statement 34, as amended?
A—Statement 34 applies to all state and local governmental entities, including general purpose governments, public school districts, public benefit corporations and authorities, public employee retirement systems, public utilities, public hospitals and other healthcare providers, and public colleges and universities. Application of the Statement 34 provisions, as amended, to public colleges and universities was achieved through the issuance of Statement No. 35, Basic Financial Statements—and Management's Discussion and Analysis—for Public Colleges and Universities.
