Chapter 6, Question 6.43.1

6.43.1.Q—A state government sponsors an investment pool for its own moneys and moneys of legally separate entities that are not component units. What are the requirements for presenting this pool in the state's annual financial report?
A—The state should report the external portion of the pool (that is, the portion belonging to the non-component-unit participants) as an investment trust fund in the fiduciary fund financial statements. (See Exhibits 41 and 42 in nonauthoritative Appendix B7-1.)
