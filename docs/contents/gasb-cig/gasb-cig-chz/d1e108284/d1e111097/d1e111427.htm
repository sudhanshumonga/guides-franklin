Chapter Z, Question Z.49.5

Z.49.5.Q—Paragraph 18 of Statement 49 requires remeasurement of a pollution remediation liability when "new information indicates changes in estimated outlays." If there are no changes in the remediation plan or operating conditions, can the liability be remeasured by simply adding an adjustment for inflation?
A—Yes. The remeasurement provisions of Statement 49 are consistent with those of Statement 18. Statement 18 illustrates costs being adjusted by inflation rates applicable to the projects.
