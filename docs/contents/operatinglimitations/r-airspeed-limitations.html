Airspeed limitations

Primary limitations are applied to takeoff, re-entry, and landing and must be observed.
Caution:

Failure to observing the limitations can result in damage to your equipment.

Warning:

Failure to observing the limitations can result in loss of life.

Action
Limit (KEAS)
Takeoff
492 maximum, no minimum stated
Re-entry
486 maximum, 163 minimum
Landing
312: lowering landing gear, 214 maximum at touchdown
