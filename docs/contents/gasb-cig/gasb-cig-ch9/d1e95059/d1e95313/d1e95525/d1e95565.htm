Chapter 9, Question 9.8.2

9.8.2.Q—Are governments engaged only in fiduciary activities required to present schedules with the information about changes in net position that other types of governmental entities prepare, in addition to those schedules specifically required in paragraph 10b of Statement 44, as amended, for governments engaged only in fiduciary activities?
A—No. The information described in paragraph 10b, as amended, is intended to be presented in lieu of the information required by paragraphs 10 and 10a of the Statement, as amended.
