Chapter 9, Question 9.24.6

9.24.6Q—Should outstanding debt and general bonded debt be presented in the schedules as gross principal outstanding or net of discounts, premiums, and other adjustments? In particular, what amount should be shown for deep-discount debt?
A—The amounts presented in the debt capacity schedules should agree with those in the basic financial statements. In other words, the amounts should include discounts, premiums, and other adjustments. Similarly, amounts shown for deep-discount debt should agree with the accreted amounts reported in the basic financial statements.
