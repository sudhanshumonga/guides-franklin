Chapter Z, Question Z.33.19

Z.33.19.Q—Are grant receivables required to be reported at their discounted present value as discussed in paragraph 180 of Statement 62?
A—No. Although Example 21a in nonauthoritative Appendix D of Statement 33 illustrates that receivables can be discounted, it does not require discounting. Paragraph 180 of Statement 62 covers accounting for receivables and payables arising from exchange transactions, not nonexchange transactions.
