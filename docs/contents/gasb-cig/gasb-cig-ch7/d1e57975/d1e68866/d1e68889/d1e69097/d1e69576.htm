Chapter 7, Question 7.56.11

7.56.11.Q—Should the major fund test be done before or after year-end adjustments have been made?
A—Major funds should be determined based on the balances that are reported in the fund financial statements—after year-end adjustments have been made. However, as a preliminary determination, it may be useful to apply the major fund criteria before adjustments so that the funds that are likely to be deemed major are identified as soon as possible in the financial reporting process.
