Chapter Z, Question Z.49.11

Z.49.11.Q—Paragraph 21 of Statement 42 states that "if not otherwise apparent in the financial statements, the amount and financial statement classification of insurance recoveries should be disclosed." Does Statement 49 supersede that disclosure requirement for insurance recoveries that reduce the measurement of pollution remediation expense and liabilities?
A—No. Paragraph 25c of Statement 49 requires disclosure of recoveries that reduce the measurement of pollution remediation liabilities. However, that disclosure does not meet the requirements of Statement 42 for two reasons. First, it does not require segregation of insurance recoveries from recoveries from other responsible parties. Second, it is a disclosure of expected recoveries rather than current-year recoveries.
