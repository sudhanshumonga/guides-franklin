Chapter Z, Question Z.49.1

Z.49.1.Q—Is a requirement to install a drinking water filtration system a pollution remediation obligation?
A—Generally, no. The requirement to install a drinking water filtration system generally would not be considered a pollution remediation obligation unless it is part of a pollution remediation activity. In the Basis for Conclusions of Statement 49, paragraph 49 notes that the scope of the Statement "excludes drinking water filtration systems and other systems whose primary purpose is to prepare resources for use rather than to conduct pollution remediation."
