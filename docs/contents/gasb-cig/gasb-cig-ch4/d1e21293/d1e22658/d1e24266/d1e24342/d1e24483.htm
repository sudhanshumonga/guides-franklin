Chapter 4, Question 4.28.6

4.28.6.Q—Is a reporting entity with no component units required to identify the columns on the financial statements as being those of a primary government?
A—No. Paragraph 42 of Statement 14 requires that the financial statements of the reporting entity distinguish between the primary government and its component units. There would be no need for identification of the columns as being those of the primary government if there are no component unit columns.
