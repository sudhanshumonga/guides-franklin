Chapter 7, Question 7.11.4

7.11.4.Q—A county has determined that a portion of the cost of a road improvement project includes nondepreciable land improvements, such as removal of existing structures and excavation. Are these costs required to be reported separately from the depreciable costs of the project?
A—Yes. The portion of the cost attributable to nondepreciable land improvements (see Question 7.9.1) should be reported with other assets not being depreciated, such as land, construction in progress, and infrastructure accounted for using the modified approach.
