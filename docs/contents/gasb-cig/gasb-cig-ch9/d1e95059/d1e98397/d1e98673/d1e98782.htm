Chapter 9, Question 9.38.4

9.38.4.Q—How many participating employers should a multiple-employer pension or other postemployment benefit plan include in the schedule of principal participating employers?
A—The basic requirement of paragraph 39c of Statement 44 is to present the 10 largest participating employers. However, if fewer than 10 employers account for 50 percent or more of total covered employees, a plan may present fewer than 10 employers. A plan may include more than 10 participating employers if it believes doing so would be meaningful and would help to meet the objectives set forth in paragraph 6 of that Statement. Some plans list all of their participating employers.
