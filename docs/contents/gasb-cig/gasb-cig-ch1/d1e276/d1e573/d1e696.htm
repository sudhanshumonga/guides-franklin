Chapter 1, Question 1.3.5

1.3.5.Q—For purposes of presenting investments by type as required by paragraph 4 of Statement 40, may sizable portions of an investment portfolio be described as "Other"?
A—No. It is not appropriate to present significant amounts of a government's portfolio in this way, unless the narrative of the note disclosure describes the composition of the "Other" or "Miscellaneous" category.
