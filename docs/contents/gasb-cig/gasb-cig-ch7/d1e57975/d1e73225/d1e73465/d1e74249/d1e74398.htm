Chapter 7, Question 7.107.7

7.107.7.Q—Paragraph 159 of Statement 34 indicates that roads and highways may be capitalized in transition using lane-miles multiplied by a standard cost. May a similar approach be used for other infrastructure assets?
A—Yes. For example, sidewalks could be estimated using square footage, curbs using lineal footage or miles, or bridges using the span footage.
