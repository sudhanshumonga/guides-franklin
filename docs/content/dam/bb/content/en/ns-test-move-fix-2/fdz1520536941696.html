Register your organization in the  management console

  1. On the menu bar, click Org Connect. The Registration screen is displayed.
  2. Click Next.
  3. In the Contact information to be shared with other organizations section, enter information for a primary contact that other organizations can view.
  4. In the Organization information section, enter you organization name and a description.
  5. In the Sector drop-down list, select the industry sector that your organization belongs to. 
  6. Optionally, click the Logo icon to add your organization's logo. Browse to and select the image file.
  7. In the Website field, enter the web address for your organization's website.
  8. In the Address  section, enter your organization's address.
  9. Click Next.
  10. In the Visibility drop-down list, select a visibility setting.
    If you select “Visible to all organizations,” all organizations can see that your organization uses , and they can request connections with you. If you select “Visible to organizations in selected sectors,” only organizations in the sectors that you specify can see that your organization uses , and they can request connections with you. If you select “Not visible,” no organizations can see that your organization uses , and they cannot request connections with you.
  11. Click Next.
    The verification screen is displayed.
