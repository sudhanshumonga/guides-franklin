Chapter 7, Question 7.106.4

7.106.4.Q—The determination of major general infrastructure assets is at the network or subsystem level. If only a portion of a network or subsystem was acquired since 1980 and no records are available to estimate the historical cost of the entire network or subsystem, how does one determine whether the asset is a major asset?
A—Only the preliminary estimate of the cost of the portion of the network or subsystem acquired or receiving major improvements in fiscal years ending after June 30, 1980, needs to be compared to the criteria for major general infrastructure assets. Retroactive reporting of assets or portions of assets acquired prior to that period is encouraged, but not required.
