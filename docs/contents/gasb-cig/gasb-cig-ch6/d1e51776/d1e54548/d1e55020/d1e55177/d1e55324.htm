Chapter 6, Question 6.43.5

6.43.5.Q—Can individual investment accounts be combined with a governmental external investment pool for reporting purposes?
A—No. The individual investment accounts should be reported as one or more separate investment trust funds. (See Questions 6.45.1–6.45.3.) Combining individual investment accounts with the investment pool might obscure information that is needed by users of the pool's financial statements.
