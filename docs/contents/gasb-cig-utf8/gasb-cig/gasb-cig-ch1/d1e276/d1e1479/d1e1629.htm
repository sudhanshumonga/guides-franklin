Chapter 1, Question 1.9.6

1.9.6.Q—A certain external investment pool is a pool that measures all of its investments at amortized cost in accordance with paragraph 4 of Statement No. 79, Accounting and Financial Reporting for Certain External Investment Pools and Pool Participants, but is not rated. Is a credit risk disclosure required?
A—Yes. An external investment pool that measures all of its investments at amortized cost is, nevertheless, an external investment pool for which paragraph 7 of Statement 40 requires a credit risk disclosure. If the pool is rated, the credit quality of the pool should be disclosed, or if the pool is unrated, the disclosure should indicate that fact.
