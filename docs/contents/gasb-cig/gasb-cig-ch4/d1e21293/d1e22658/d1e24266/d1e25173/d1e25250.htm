Chapter 4, Question 4.30.2

4.30.2.Q—Can professional judgment be used as a basis for blending a component unit that does not meet the criteria for blending?
A—No. Only those component units that meet the criteria in paragraph 53 of Statement 14, as amended, should be reported using the blending method. However, organizations that meet the criteria in paragraph 5 of Statement 39 should be discretely presented, even if they also meet the criterion in paragraph 53b or paragraph 53c of Statement 14, as amended or paragraph 4 of Statement 80.
