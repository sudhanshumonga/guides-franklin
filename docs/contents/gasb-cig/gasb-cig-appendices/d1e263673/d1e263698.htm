Appendix B1-2: Statements 3 and 40, as Amended—Illustrative Financial Statement Disclosures
The sample disclosures included in this appendix are presented to illustrate certain disclosure requirements of Statements No. 3, Deposits with Financial Institutions, Investments (including Repurchase Agreements), and Reverse Repurchase Agreements, and No. 40, Deposit and Investment Risk Disclosures, as amended.
Other disclosure requirements, not covered in these illustrations, apply. For example, a summary of significant accounting policies, investments authorized by legal or contractual provisions, significant violations of legal or contractual provisions, and a government's steps taken to address such violations are required disclosures discussed in other authoritative accounting literature. National Council on Governmental Accounting Interpretation 6, Notes to the Financial Statements Disclosure, as amended, and GASB Statement 38, Certain Financial Statement Note Disclosures, as amended, describe many of those disclosure requirements.
