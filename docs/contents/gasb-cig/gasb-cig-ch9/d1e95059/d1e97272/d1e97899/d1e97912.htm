Chapter 9, Question 9.30.1

9.30.1.Q—Statement 44 limits the deductions from the debt amounts used to calculate ratios of general bonded debt. (See Question 9.28.2.) Does this limitation also apply to the schedule of debt limitations?
A—No. The calculation of the debt limit should be based on the legal requirements under which a government operates. If the law stipulating how the debt limit is calculated allows for the deduction of amounts other than resources restricted to repaying principal, then the calculation in this schedule should show those deductions.
