Chapter 9, Question 9.49.1

9.49.1.Q—Should the individual years be presented in columns, with the earliest year on the left and the most recent year on the right, as illustrated in Statement 44? Is a government required to present the individual years in columns, or may they be presented in rows instead?
A—As long as the required information is presented, governments may order the information in any fashion that they, in their professional judgment, deem to be most effective.
