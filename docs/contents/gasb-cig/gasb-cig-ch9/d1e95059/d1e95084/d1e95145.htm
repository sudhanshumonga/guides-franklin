Chapter 9, Question 9.2.3

9.2.3.Q—If a government publishes a statistical section separately from its financial report, should it apply Statement 44?
A—A government may apply Statement 44 to a freestanding statistical section but is not required to do so. However, the term statistical section is governmental accounting terminology that conveys certain expectations about the information that will be presented, based on the requirements of governmental accounting and financial reporting standards. Consequently, a government that does not apply Statement 44 may wish to consider giving its document a different title to avoid misleading the users of the document.
