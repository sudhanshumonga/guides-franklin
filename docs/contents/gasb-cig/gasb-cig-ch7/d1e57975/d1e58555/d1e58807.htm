Chapter 7, Question 7.5.8

7.5.8.Q—Paragraph 11 of Statement 34, as amended, explains what should be in MD&A and states that only the most relevant information should be included. Based upon that notion, should paragraph 11d be interpreted to mean that only major funds are required to be addressed?
A—The analysis of balances and transactions of individual funds, as required by paragraph 11d, would normally be confined to major funds. Nevertheless, governments can discuss transactions or significant changes in account balances of nonmajor funds.
