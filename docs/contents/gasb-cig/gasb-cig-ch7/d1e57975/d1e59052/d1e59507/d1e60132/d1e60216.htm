Chapter 7, Question 7.12.4

7.12.4.Q—If one government is responsible for maintaining a capital asset and a different government is responsible for replacing the same asset, which government should report the capital asset?
A—When ownership is unclear, the government with the responsibility for managing the asset should report it. In this circumstance, the government that maintains the asset is the one that has responsibility for managing the asset and, therefore, should report the asset.
