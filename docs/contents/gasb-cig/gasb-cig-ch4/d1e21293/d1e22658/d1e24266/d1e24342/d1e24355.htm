Chapter 4, Question 4.28.1

4.28.1.Q—What criteria should be used to determine whether a component unit is discretely presented or blended?
A—Component units that meet the criteria in paragraph 53 of Statement 14, as amended, should be blended. However, excluding component units that are fiduciary in nature, most component units, including those that meet the criteria of paragraph 5 of Statement 39, should be discretely presented. (See Question 4.30.1 for a discussion of the blending criteria.)
