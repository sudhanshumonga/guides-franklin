Chapter 7, Question 7.50.10

7.50.10.Q—A city operates an electric department, a water and sewer department, and a natural gas department. Each utility operation has issued bonds that are repayable solely from the revenues of the respective operations. Are separate enterprise funds required for each of those departments?
A—No. Paragraph 67(a) of Statement 34 requires only that an enterprise fund be used to report the activities but does not require separate funds for each activity. However, each utility operation may be required to be reported separately in the government-wide statement of activities if they satisfy the criteria in paragraph 10(b) of Statement 37, as different identifiable activities. In addition, disclosure of segment information, as discussed in paragraph 122 of Statement 34, as amended, may be required.
