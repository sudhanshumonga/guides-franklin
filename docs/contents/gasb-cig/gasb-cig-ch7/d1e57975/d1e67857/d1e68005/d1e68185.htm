Chapter 7, Question 7.50.5

7.50.5.Q—A municipality does not have a formal "pricing policy," per se, for its water and sewer operation, but it does adopt an annual operating budget in which fees and charges are established to fully recover all budgeted costs, including an amount to cover depreciation of the water and sewer capital assets. Is enterprise fund accounting required for the water and sewer fund?
A—Yes. The adoption of the operating budget in this scenario produces a rate schedule that meets the intent of paragraph 67c of Statement 34. The formally adopted budget is equivalent to a pricing policy.
