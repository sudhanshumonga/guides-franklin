Chapter 7, Question 7.23.13

7.23.13.Q—Should construction retainages payable be considered capital-related debt for purposes of calculating the net investment in capital assets component of net position?
A—A retainage payable represents a liability attributable to the acquisition, construction, or improvement of capital assets (in this case, construction in progress) and retainage liabilities should be included in the calculation of the net investment in capital assets component of net position. (See also Question 7.23.14 about projects financed with bond proceeds.)
