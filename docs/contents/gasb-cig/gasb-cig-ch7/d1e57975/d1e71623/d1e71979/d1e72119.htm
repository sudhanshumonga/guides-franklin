Chapter 7, Question 7.86.5

7.86.5.Q—A city operates under an internal policy that requires separate accounting for assets, deferred outflows of resources, liabilities, deferred inflows of resources, revenues, and expenses related to any activity that raises revenues pledged as security for debt. Is the city required to disclose segment information relative to those activities?
A—No. Paragraph 17 of Statement 37, as amended, clarifies that the separate accounting requirement should be imposed by an external party. The city should not include its "segment-like" information in the required segment disclosure, but may present the information in a separate note or as supplementary information without referring to the condensed financial information as "segment" information.
