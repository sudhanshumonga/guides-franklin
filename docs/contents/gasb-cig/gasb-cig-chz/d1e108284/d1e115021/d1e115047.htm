Chapter Z, Question Z.63.1

Z.63.1.Q—Can a deferred outflow of resources be combined with a deferred inflow of resources and reported as a net amount if the underlying transactions are similar? For example, can a deferred outflow of resources arising from an effective hedging arrangement be combined with a deferred inflow of resources arising from an effective hedging arrangement?
A—Paragraph 7 of Statement 63 requires presentation in separate sections. In the example in this question, unless there is a specific right of offset provision in the hedging arrangements, the deferred amounts should be classified separately. (See Questions 5.142.3 and 5.142.4 [for single and agent employers], Questions 5.165.1, 5.183.3, and 5.183.4 [for cost-sharing employers], Question 5.200.1 [for single and agent employers that have a special funding situation], and Question 5.219.1 [for governmental nonemployer contributing entities in a special funding situation] for discussion of the application of the provisions of Statement 68, as amended, with regard to reporting deferred outflows of resources and deferred inflows of resources related to pensions.)
