Operating limitations

There are a wide set of limitations and the User Guide only lists a few key ones you need to know. Ensure you review the Flight Operations Manual.
Caution:

Limitations are the operational limits, which if exceeded, affect safety or result in performance loss.

Limitations discuss those for a specific vehicle, but are not all inclusive. The flight crew must ensure they are familiar with all limitations and follow all instructions.

If flying on approach when returning, the flight crew must be sure they maintain minimum speeds based on conditions. They need to know what approach to take based on a specific landing site. All of this is dependent on the vehicle, landing site setup, and weather conditions.
