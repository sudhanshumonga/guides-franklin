Chapter 1, Question 1.57.8

1.57.8.Q—Should the financial statements of an external investment pool that measures all of its investments at amortized cost in accordance with paragraph 4 of Statement 79 disclose the interest rate risk of its debt investments?
A—Yes. The interest rate risk disclosures required by paragraphs 14–16 of Statement 40, as amended, apply to all external investment pools, including pools that measure all of their investments at amortized cost in accordance with paragraph 4 of Statement 79.
