Chapter Z, Question Z.54.55

Z.54.55.Q—A county government accounts for the proceeds of a federal grant in a capital projects fund. The grant proceeds are restricted to highway construction, and no other resources are accounted for in that fund. The board of supervisors takes the required highest level action to commit a portion of the resources in the fund to a specific construction project. How should the amount committed to that project be classified?
A—The entire fund balance should be classified as restricted because it arises from the proceeds of a restricted grant. To classify a portion as committed understates the constraint imposed on the use of those resources by the grant agreement.
