Chapter 4, Question 4.43.3

4.43.3.Q—Do the provisions of Statement 14, as amended, apply to the separately issued financial statements of a nongovernmental organization that is a component unit?
A—No. A nongovernmental component unit would not apply the provisions of Statement 14, as amended, to its separately issued financial statements; however, Statement 14, as amended, would be applied to the nongovernmental component unit when it is included in the financial statements of the governmental reporting entity. (See Question 4.33.3.)
