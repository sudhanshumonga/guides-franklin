Chapter 2, Question 2.20.3

2.20.3.Q—How should contributions received by an enterprise fund that are not intended for capital purposes be classified?
A—Paragraph 21b of Statement 9 defines "noncapital" grants and subsidies as noncapital financing activities. A noncapital contribution to an enterprise fund would normally be used the same as a grant or subsidy; therefore, it should be classified as a noncapital financing inflow. (See Example I in nonauthoritative Appendix B2-1.)
