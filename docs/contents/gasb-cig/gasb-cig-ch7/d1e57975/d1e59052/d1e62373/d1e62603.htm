Chapter 7, Question 7.22.8

7.22.8.Q—Net position is reported in three components. (See Question 7.22.7.) Paragraph 8 of Statement 63 and paragraph 35 of Statement 34, as amended, also set forth reporting requirements for supporting details of restricted net position. Can those details (major categories, expendable/nonexpendable) be provided in the notes?
A—No. Paragraph 8 of Statement 63 and paragraph 35 of Statement 34, as amended, both state that the information should be displayed; therefore, disclosure is not an option. However, paragraph 6 of Statement No. 46, Net Assets Restricted by Enabling Legislation, as amended, requires that the amount of net position restricted by enabling legislation be disclosed in the notes to the financial statements. (See Question 7.23.10 about disclosing unrestricted net position details that are not required.)
