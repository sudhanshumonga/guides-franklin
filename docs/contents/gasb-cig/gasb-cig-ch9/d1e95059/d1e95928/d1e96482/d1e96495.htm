Chapter 9, Question 9.16.1

9.16.1.Q—State and regional governments are not required to present the revenue rates of overlapping governments (although regional governments are encouraged to do so). Do they have to present a revenue rate schedule?
A—Yes. Even if a government is not required to report the rates of overlapping governments, it should report each individual direct rate it levies or charges for its most significant own-source revenue, as required by paragraph 16 of Statement 44.
