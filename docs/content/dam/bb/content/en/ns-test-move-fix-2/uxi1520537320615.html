Search for an organization

You can search for an organization by organization name or location on the All organizations page. The organization that you are searching for must already be registered in  and be visible to all organizations or the sector that your organization belongs to. If the organization you are searching for is not registered, you can invite them as an unlisted organization. 
  1. On the menu bar, click Org Connect > All organizations.
  2. In the drop-down list beside the search field, select one of the following options:
    • Organization name
    • Location
  3. In the Search field, enter your search criteria
  4. Press Enter.
