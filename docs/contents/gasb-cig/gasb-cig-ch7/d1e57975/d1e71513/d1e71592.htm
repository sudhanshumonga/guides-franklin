Chapter 7, Question 7.82.3

7.82.3.Q—How should a government report a "payment in lieu of taxes" from its enterprise utility fund to its general fund?
A—The payment would be reported either as an interfund transfer or as interfund services provided and used—that is, as an expense in the enterprise fund and a revenue in the general fund. As discussed in paragraph 112, the distinction is based on whether the payment in lieu of taxes is a payment for, and reasonably equivalent in value to, the services provided by the general fund. (See also Question 7.72.7.)
