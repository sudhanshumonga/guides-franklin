Chapter 2, Question 2.15.5

2.15.5.Q—If a certificate of deposit that does not meet the definition of a cash equivalent automatically rolls over at maturity, is there a cash flow?
A—No. A cash flow does not occur unless there is a deposit to or withdrawal from cash or a cash equivalent. Therefore, a rollover does not affect the statement of cash flows. The certificate of deposit is merely converted from one investment instrument to another.
