Chapter 6, Question 6.44.2

6.44.2.Q—Does Statement 31 mandate the manner, method, or frequency of the distribution of investment income?
A—No. The distribution of investment income is the determination of the pool sponsor in conjunction with pool participants. Regardless of the distribution of investment income, however, pool participants should value their position in the pool using fair value. The only exceptions are investment positions in open-end mutual funds and in pools that measure all of their investments at amortized cost in accordance with paragraph 4 of Statement 79, which are reported using the fund's share price or pool's amortized cost-based net asset value per share because it approximates fair value.
