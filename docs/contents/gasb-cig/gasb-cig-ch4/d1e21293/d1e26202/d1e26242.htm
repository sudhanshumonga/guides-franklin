Chapter 4, Question 4.39.2

4.39.2.Q—Are individual component unit disclosures required for all discretely presented components?
A—No. Paragraph 51 of Statement 14, as amended, requires, in certain instances, disclosure of information about each “major” component unit included in the component units column(s). The nature and significance of its relationship to the primary government should be considered to determine which component units are “major.” (See Question 4.28.17 for further discussion of major component units and Question 4.39.4 for disclosure of how component units are reported.)
