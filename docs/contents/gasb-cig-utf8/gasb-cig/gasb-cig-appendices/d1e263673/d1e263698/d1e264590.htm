Illustration 9—Investments with Fair Values That Are Highly Sensitive to Interest Rate Changes
Investments with Fair Values That Are Highly Sensitive to Interest Rate Changes
The disclosures illustrated here address the requirements of Statement 40, paragraph 16. Depending on the method used to communicate interest rate risk (Statement 40, paragraphs 14 and 15)—for example, duration—additional interest rate risk disclosures may be necessary. Other disclosures may be required, such as credit risk, concentration of credit risk, and derivative instrument-specific disclosures (Statement 53, paragraphs 69 and 79a) for investments that are derivative instruments.
