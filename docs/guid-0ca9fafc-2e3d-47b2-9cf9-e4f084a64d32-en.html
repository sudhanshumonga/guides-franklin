Submit your  registration 

After you set visibility for your organization, you can review the information you entered before you send it to  for verification. 
  1. In the Organization visibility section, verify the selection.
  2. In the Organization information section, verify the information about your organization.
  3. Click Submit.
The verification screen is displayed. When your organization information has been verified, you receive an email with instructions to log in to .
