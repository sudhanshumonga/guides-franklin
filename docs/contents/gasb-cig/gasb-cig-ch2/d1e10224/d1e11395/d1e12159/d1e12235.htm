Chapter 2, Question 2.24.3

2.24.3.Q—How should capital contributed by developers and other individuals be classified?
A—Cash contributed specifically to defray the cost of acquiring, constructing, or improving capital assets should be presented in the capital and related financing activities category, as required by paragraph 24c of Statement 9. Noncash transactions (such as the contribution of water and sewer lines by a developer) should be disclosed in a schedule to the statement of cash flows. (See Questions 2.32.1 and 2.32.2; see also Examples A–C and I in nonauthoritative Appendix B2-1.)
