Cabin escape

The cabin can be evacuated through a self-enclosed ejection system.

This system will result in quick depressurization of the cabin to match the surrounding pressure (so in space it will completely vacate the air from the cabin, but within the atmosphere it will normalize to the outside pressure).

The order to escape from the cabin is given by the flight crew. This could be while the vehicle is still on the ground, or it can be done in space.
