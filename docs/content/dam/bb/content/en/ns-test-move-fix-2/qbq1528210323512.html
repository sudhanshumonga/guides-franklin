Cancel a pending connection request

You can cancel a connection request before it has been accepted by an organization.
  1. On the menu bar, click Org Connect > Mailbox.
  2. Click Sent to view your sent items.
  3. In the message list, click the connection request that you want to cancel.
  4. In the Connection request section, click Cancel.
  5. Click Cancel.
After you cancel the connection request, the  owner in the invited organization receives a notification in the  mailbox and by email.
