Chapter 7, Question 7.23.5

7.23.5.Q—Often, debt is issued for capital purposes, but some of the proceeds are spent for assets that are not capitalized. Should some of the debt be removed from the net investment in capital assets component of net position?
A—Governments are not expected to categorize all uses of bond proceeds to determine how much of the debt actually relates to assets that have been capitalized. Unless a significant portion of the debt proceeds is spent for noncapitalizable purposes, the entire amount could be considered "capital-related."
