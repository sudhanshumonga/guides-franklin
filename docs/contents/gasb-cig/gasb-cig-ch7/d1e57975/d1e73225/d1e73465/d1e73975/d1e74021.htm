Chapter 7, Question 7.105.2

7.105.2.Q—When implementing the modified approach, at least one condition assessment should be available. (See paragraph 152 of Statement 34.) If a government's most recent condition assessment for an infrastructure asset was completed nearly three years prior to the date at which the modified approach is being adopted, including instances when infrastructure acquired prior to the adoption of Statement 34 is being reported, would this asset qualify for the modified approach?
A—Yes. Condition assessments should be completed within a three-year period.
