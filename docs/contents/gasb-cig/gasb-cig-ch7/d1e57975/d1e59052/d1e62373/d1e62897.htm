Chapter 7, Question 7.22.17

7.22.17.Q—Is a change in capitalization threshold a change in accounting principle that requires restatement of beginning net position?
A—No. Use of a capitalization threshold is an application of accounting policy, not a change in accounting principle. Changes in capitalization threshold involve many considerations such as ensuring that all significant capital assets, collectively, are capitalized while considering the cost of record keeping for capital assets.
