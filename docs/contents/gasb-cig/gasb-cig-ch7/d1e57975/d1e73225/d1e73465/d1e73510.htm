Chapter 7, Question 7.104.2

7.104.2.Q—A primary government qualified for phase 1 implementation and retroactively applied the major general infrastructure reporting provisions. If the primary government has a discretely presented component unit that qualified for phase 3 implementation, was that component unit also required to retroactively apply those infrastructure requirements?
A—No. The component unit would have been required to implement Statement 34 at the same time as the primary government, but the exemption provided to phase 3 governments in paragraph 148 of Statement 34 is not affected by the reporting requirements of the primary government.
