Chapter Z, Question Z.47.2

Z.47.2.Q—An employer provides an early retirement incentive in the form of an enhanced benefit formula for defined benefit pension benefits. What effect does this have on amounts reported by the employer with regard to its defined benefit pension arrangements?
A—In addition to complying with the note disclosure requirements of Statement 47, as amended, employers that provide termination benefits that affect a defined benefit pension plan are required by paragraph 17 of Statement 47, as amended, to account for and report the effects of the termination benefits in accordance with the requirements of Statement 68, as amended, or Statement 73, as applicable. Therefore, the effect of the termination benefit would be reflected in all applicable pension measures reported by the employer, including amounts recognized in the financial statements, those disclosed in notes, and those presented in schedules of RSI.
