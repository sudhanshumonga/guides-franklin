2.21.3.Q—How should interest on cash and cash equivalents be classified?
A—Interest earnings, by nature, are an investing activity; therefore, interest on cash and cash equivalents should be considered an investing activity. The discussion in the answer to Question 2.21.2 is relevant to this question. Even though cash and cash equivalents are not investments, the interest they earn should be classified the same as interest earned on investments.
