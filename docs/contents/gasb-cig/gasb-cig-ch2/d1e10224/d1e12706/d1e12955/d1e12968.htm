Chapter 2, Question 2.30.1

2.30.1.Q—Can the reconciliation of operating income to net cash flow from operating activities be presented in notes to the financial statements?
A—No. The reconciliation is a requirement of cash flow reporting and should be presented as part of the statement of cash flows, as a separate schedule (preferably on the same page). Paragraph 67 of the Statement 9 Basis for Conclusions discusses this topic.
