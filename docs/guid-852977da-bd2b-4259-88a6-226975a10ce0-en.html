Edit visibility for your organization

When you set your organization's visibility, you specify which organizations that are registered on the  network can view your organization's profile and request connections.
Register your organization in the management console.
  1. On the menu bar, click Org Connect > Organization profile.
  2. Click .
  3. In the Visibility drop-down list, select one of the following options:
    • Visible to all organizations: All organizations can see that your organization uses , and they can request connections with you. 
    • Visible to organizations in selected sectors: Only organizations in the sectors that you specify can see that your organization uses , and they can request connections with you.
    • Not visible: No organizations can see that your organization uses , and they cannot request connections with you.
  4. Click Save.
