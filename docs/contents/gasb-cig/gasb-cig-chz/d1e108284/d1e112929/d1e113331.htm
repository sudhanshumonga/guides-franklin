Chapter Z, Question Z.54.2

Z.54.2.Q—Paragraph 6 of Statement 54 states that long-term receivables and property held for resale should not be included in the calculation of nonspendable fund balance if the proceeds from their collection or sale are restricted, committed, or assigned. Does that provision also apply to other nonspendable items such as inventories and prepaid amounts?
A—Generally, no. Inventories and prepaid amounts reported in governmental funds are examples of nonspendable items that are not expected to be converted to cash, so it is expected that there would not be any restricted, committed, or assigned collections or sale proceeds associated with inventories or prepaid amounts.
