Chapter 6, Question 6.45.3

6.45.3.Q—A county government provides individual investment accounts to other, legally separate special-purpose governments. Those governments are not part of the county's reporting entity. In the combining and individual fund financial statements, can the county aggregate all individual investment accounts for these governments?
A—Yes. In the combining and individual fund financial statements, all individual investment accounts may be aggregated as a single investment trust fund and be presented as one column.
