The following are nonauthoritative examples of the calculations that, if applicable, are required by paragraphs 13f(1) and 13f(2) of Statement 45. When components of the total unfunded actuarial liability are separately amortized over different periods, the individual periods are required to be selected so that the equivalent single amortization period for all components combined does not exceed the maximum acceptable amortization period. An equivalent single amortization period is a weighted average period calculated in accordance with paragraph 13f(2) of Statement 45. For these illustrations, the maximum acceptable period is 30 years. The data included in the examples are hypothetical and are not intended to indicate endorsement of the amortization periods and methods shown. The amortization factors are based on the actuarial assumptions and the amortization method and period. Use of a different formula for calculating factors could produce slightly different factors.
