Chapter Z, Question Z.48.7

Z.48.7.Q—Paragraph 12 of Statement 48, as amended, states that "collections of the pledged revenues or receivables that are subsequently paid to the transferee reduce the liability in the pledging government’s statements of net position." Does that requirement apply to all collateralized debt?
A—No. The requirement in paragraph 12 is intended to apply to those situations in which the government receives resources in exchange for future cash flows but does not issue a debt instrument.
