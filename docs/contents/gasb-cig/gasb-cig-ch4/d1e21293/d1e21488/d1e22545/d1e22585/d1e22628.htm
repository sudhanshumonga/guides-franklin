Chapter 4, Question 4.14.2

4.14.2.Q—Does Statement 14 change the reporting requirements for fiduciary relationships in which the primary government has a fiduciary responsibility to another primary government, such as when a state performs treasury services for another primary government?
A—No. Paragraph 19, as amended, specifically states that the standard was not intended to modify the fund reporting requirements of the primary government, including those for fiduciary funds. Therefore, a primary government that provides such services would continue to report those balances in the appropriate fiduciary funds.
