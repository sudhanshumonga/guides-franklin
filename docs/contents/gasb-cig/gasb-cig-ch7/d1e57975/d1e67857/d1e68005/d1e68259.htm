Chapter 7, Question 7.50.8

7.50.8.Q—Should administrative costs be reported in a state's unemployment compensation enterprise fund?
A—No. That activity should be included in the general fund unless legal requirements exist that require the accounting and financial reporting of the resources in another fund, as required by NCGA Interpretation 9, paragraph 9, as amended. The administrative activity is not required to be accounted for in the unemployment compensation enterprise fund—such a requirement would invalidate the reasoning that an enterprise fund is required, as noted in footnote 34 of Statement 34. The charges are not designed to recover the costs of administration.
