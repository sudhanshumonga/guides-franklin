Chapter 9, Question 9.47.2

9.47.2.Q—Are governments required to present the table of contents for the statistical section?
A—No. However, a government might utilize narrative explanations, such as a table of contents, to help a user understand the nature of statistical section information and make it easier for the reader to use. Exhibit A-1 in nonauthoritative Appendix B9-2 provides an illustration of narrative explanations.
