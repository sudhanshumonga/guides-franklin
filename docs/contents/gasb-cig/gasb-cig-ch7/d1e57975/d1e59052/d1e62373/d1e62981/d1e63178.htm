Chapter 7, Question 7.23.7

7.23.7.Q—If a government has capital assets, but no related debt or deferred inflows of resources, may the net position account be titled "investment in capital assets"?
A—Yes. The net position title "net investment in capital assets" denotes that there is capital debt or deferred inflows of resources and may mislead readers if used when there is no debt or deferred inflows of resources.
