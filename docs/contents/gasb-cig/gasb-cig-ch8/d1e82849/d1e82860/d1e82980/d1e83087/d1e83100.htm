Chapter 8, Question 8.4.1

8.4.1.Q—If no OPEB plan (in the sense of a trust, or equivalent arrangement), has been established, and the employer does not contribute more than the pay-as-you-go requirement each year (that is, no plan net position is being accumulated), is the employer required to apply the measurement and recognition requirements of Statement 45, as amended, if it is providing defined benefit postemployment healthcare benefits?
A—Yes. The requirements of Statement 45, as amended, apply regardless of how or when OPEB is financed. (Also see the discussion in Question 8.1.2 regarding the usage of the term plan in Statement 45.)
