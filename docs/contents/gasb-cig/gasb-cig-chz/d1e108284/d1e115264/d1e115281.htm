Chapter Z, Question Z.70.1

Z.70.1.Q—If a blended component unit recognizes a receivable from the primary government relating to a nonexchange financial guarantee, should the component unit evaluate that receivable for collectability and recognize an allowance, if necessary?
A—Paragraph 13 of Statement 70 requires the government that issued the guaranteed obligation to recognize a receivable equal to the amount of the liability recognized by the government that extended the guarantee. However, the blended component unit should continue to evaluate the collectability of the receivable.
