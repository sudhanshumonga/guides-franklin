Chapter 4, Question 4.18.9

4.18.9.Q—If the board of a primary government's component unit appoints a voting majority of the members of another organization's governing body and is able to impose its will on that organization, is that organization a component unit of the primary government?
A—No. The other organization is not a component unit of the primary government, per se; however, the effect on the reporting entity's financial statements would be the same based on the "bottom-up" application in paragraph 43 of Statement 14.
