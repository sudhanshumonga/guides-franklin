Chapter 2, Question 2.29.5

2.29.5.Q—Should debt service interest payments that are capitalized be reported in the capital and related financing activities category as an outflow for interest on capital debt or as part of the capital acquisition outflow?
A—All interest payments associated with "capital debt" should be presented in the capital and related financing activities category as "cash payments to lenders and other creditors for interest." The presentation of cash payments for interest should not be affected by the transaction's presentation on the operating statement or the statement of net position/balance sheet. The fact that the amount of the cash flow is capitalized does not change the fact that the cash was paid to a lender for interest.
