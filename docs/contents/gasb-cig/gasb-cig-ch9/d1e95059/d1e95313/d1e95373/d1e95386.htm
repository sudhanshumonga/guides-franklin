Chapter 9, Question 9.7.1

9.7.1.Q—Is it acceptable to present expenses by object—such as salaries, fringe benefits, supplies, contractual services, and so on—instead of by function, program, or identifiable activity?
A—No. Expenses by object may be a useful complement to the presentation of expenses by function or program. However, it is not an acceptable substitute for the required functional, programmatic, or identifiable activity categorization of expenses.
