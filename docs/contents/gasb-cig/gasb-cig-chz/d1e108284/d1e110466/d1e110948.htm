Chapter Z, Question Z.48.12

Z.48.12.Q—Regarding the example in Question Z.48.11, are the pledged revenue disclosures required to be made in the state college’s financial statements, which include the blended component unit?
A—No. The college has not pledged a specific revenue to make the rent payments to the component unit that indirectly collateralize or secure the debt of the component unit.
