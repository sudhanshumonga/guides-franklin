Chapter 9, Question 9.43.1

9.43.1.Q—Can any of the required information be presented in a graphical format—such as a bar or pie chart—instead of in the tabular format?
A—No. Graphics can be a valuable complement to tabular information, highlighting important information and trends for the user of the financial report. However, the requirements of Statement 44 can be met only with the presentation of tabular information.
