Audit and logging

On the audit and logging page, you can view a list of actions that administrators have performed on your connected apps, your organizaton's network, and your organization's profile and settings. The page displays the date and time an event occurred, the administrative role and user that performed the action, a category, and the type of action that was performed. For actions related to app connections, the name of the connected organization and the app are also displayed.
