Chapter 4, Question 4.16.2

4.16.2.Q—Should an organization that is not legally separate be considered a potential component unit?
A—No. An organization that is not legally separate, as defined by Statement 14, should not be considered a potential component unit. It should be reported in the same manner as departments or agencies of the primary government that holds the organization's corporate powers. (See Question 4.8.8.)
