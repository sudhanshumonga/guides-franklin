1.9.4.Q—Do all positions with credit exposure need to be listed separately? For example, how would a large portfolio disclose the credit quality rating of its investments?
A—To avoid unreasonably long disclosures, a government may choose to present aggregated credit risk disclosures for its portfolio of investments. Such disclosures may include the percentage of the portfolio constituted by each of the investment types with different categories of ratings. See Illustration 6 in nonauthoritative Appendix B1-2.
