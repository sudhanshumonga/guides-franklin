Adding an email account with manual setup

Depending on the email provider you use, you might have to manually set up your email account in the . You might also need to use manual setup if your email provider or administrator uses a specific email configuration.

With manual setup, you can add a POP3, IMAP, or  account. If you don't know what type of email account you have, contact your email provider.

Add a POP3 or IMAP email account

  1. In the , tap  > Add account.
  2. Type your email address.
  3. Tap Manual setup.
  4. Tap your email account type:
    • POP3
    • IMAP
  5. On the Incoming server settings screen, enter the following info:
    • In the Password field, enter your email account password.
    • In the Server field, enter your email provider's server address.
    • In the Port field, enter the port number.
    • If necessary, in the Security type drop-down list, select a security type.
  6. Tap Next.
  7. On the Outgoing server settings screen, enter the following info:
    • In the SMTP server field, enter your email provider's SMTP server address.
    • In the Port field, enter the port number.
    • If necessary, in the Security type drop-down list, select a security type.
    • In the Password field, enter your email account password.
  8. Tap Next.
  9. On the Account settings screen, review or change any of the settings.
  10. Tap Next.
  11. Optionally, change the display name or display color for your email account.
  12. Tap Done.
To find the server and configuration settings that your email account uses, contact your email provider.
