Chapter 3, Question 3.48.1

3.48.1.Q—What is an “entity other than a pool”?
A—For the purposes of Statement 10, an entity other than a pool is any state or local governmental entity financing risk that does not meet the definition of a public entity risk pool in paragraphs 10–13. (See Question 3.4.1.) The state or local governmental entity may be a general government (such as a state, county, or municipality), a public benefit corporation or authority, a public employee retirement system, a utility, a hospital, or a college or university.
