Chapter Z, Question Z.38.1

Z.38.1.Q—Under Statement No. 13, Accounting for Operating Leases with Scheduled Rent Increases, the amounts reported as rental expense for a period may be different than the cash payments required by the lease. Should the disclosure of capital and noncancelable operating lease obligations, required by Statement 38, be based on the payments to be made or on the amounts to be expensed?
A—Statement 38 requires disclosure of “future minimum [lease] payments.” Therefore, the disclosure should be based on the cash payment requirements of the lease.
