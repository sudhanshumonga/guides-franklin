Chapter 7, Question 7.72.9

7.72.9.Q—A special-purpose government that reports as a BTA transfers title to capital assets or makes capital grants to another governmental entity. How should these transactions be recorded on the statement of revenues, expenses, and changes in net position?
A—The government that transfers title to capital assets to another government is, in substance, making a capital grant to the receiving government. In the statement of revenues, expenses, and changes in net position, the reporting should be the same regardless of whether the resources are capital assets or financial resources restricted to the acquisition of capital assets. In either case, the government should not report the transaction as an operating expense (unless the principal activity of the entity is making capital grants).
