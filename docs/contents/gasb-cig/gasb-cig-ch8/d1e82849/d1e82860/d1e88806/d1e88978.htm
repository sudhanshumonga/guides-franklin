Chapter 8, Question 8.47.7

8.47.7.Q—What information is needed to make a projection using the alternative measurement method?
A—To apply the alternative measurement method, an employer should gather information about the terms of the OPEB plan, demographic data about plan members (and spouses, dependents, and other beneficiaries, if covered by the terms of the plan), and information to develop the assumptions (including the inflation, investment return, and healthcare cost trend rate assumptions) relevant to the plan terms.
