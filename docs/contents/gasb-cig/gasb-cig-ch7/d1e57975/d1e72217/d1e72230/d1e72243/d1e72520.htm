Chapter 7, Question 7.91.10

7.91.10.Q—The requirement in paragraph 130 of Statement 34, as amended, for reporting budgetary comparison schedules refers to "legally adopted annual budgets." Are governments that budget on a biennial basis exempt from this requirement?
A—No. The budgetary comparison reporting requirements also apply to governments with biennial budgets. Paragraph 18 of NCGA Interpretation 10 includes biennial budgets as an example of timing differences.
