Chapter 9, Question 9.24.1

9.24.1.Q—Should the debt capacity schedules include long-term obligations such as compensated absences, net pension liabilities, landfill postclosure costs, or claims and judgments?
A—No. For the purposes of the statistical section, debt is limited to long-term debt instruments, including (but not limited to) bonds, notes, certificates of participation, loans, and capital leases. However, a government may present information about other long-term obligations in a separate schedule if it believes the information will be useful to the user of its financial report and will help to achieve the objectives set forth in paragraph 6 of Statement 44. (See Exhibit C-12 in nonauthoritative Appendix B9-2 for an example.)
