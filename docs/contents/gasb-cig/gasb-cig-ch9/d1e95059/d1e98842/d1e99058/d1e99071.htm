Chapter 9, Question 9.44.1

9.44.1.Q—May a government include percentage change or percentage distribution ratios in its schedules?
A—Yes. Percentage change and percentage distribution ratios are staples of financial analysis and may help to make the statistical section schedules more understandable to a user. Governments also may find that presenting these ratios with the required statistical section information is helpful in achieving the objectives set forth in paragraph 6 of Statement 44. Exhibits D-17 and E-4 in nonauthoritative Appendix B9-2 include examples of the use of these ratios. Although these ratios may be a valuable complement to the statistical section schedules, they may not be presented in lieu of the actual information required by Statement 44.
