Chapter 7, Question 7.24.13

7.24.13.Q—Can restricted net position be reported as a negative amount?
A—No. Negative amounts should not be reported for any category of restricted net position. If liabilities and deferred inflows of resources that relate to specific restricted assets exceed those assets, no restricted component of net position should be reported—the net negative amount should reduce unrestricted net position. Restricted net position is intended to portray, at the date of the statement of net position, the extent to which the government has assets that can only be used for specific purposes (after recognizing the specific liabilities that will be liquidated with those assets, or the specific liabilities and deferred inflows of resources from which the assets resulted). If the related liabilities and deferred inflows of resources exceed the assets on hand, the "shortfall" by default is covered by unrestricted assets. (See also Questions 7.24.7 and 7.24.8 Exercise 3 in nonauthoritative Appendix B7-3.)
