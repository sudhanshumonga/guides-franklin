Chapter Z, Question Z.54.29

Z.54.29.Q—Are governments required to disclose reasons why fund balance may have decreased below the established minimum or what corrective measures will be taken to restore it to the minimum level?
A—No. Paragraph 27 of Statement 54 only requires note disclosure of the policy that sets forth the minimum amount. The government should consider whether to discuss those issues in MD&A in the context of discussing significant changes in fund balances.
