NOTE 4—INTERFUND BALANCES AND ACTIVITY

Note 4—Interfund Balances and Activity
4-A.Balances Due to/from Other Funds
Balances due to/from other funds at June 30, 20X2, consist of the following:
The balance of $2,070,528 due to the General Fund from the Sales Tax Revenue Fund is for sales tax revenue collected but not yet remitted to the General Fund for health and welfare programs. $1,921,169 due to the General Fund from other governmental funds represents short-term loans. $1,094,885 is due from the General Fund to other governmental funds for capital project expenditures.
4-B.Transfers to/from Other Funds
Transfers to/from other funds at June 30, 20X2, consist of the following:
An amount of $37,208,484 was transferred from the Sales Tax Revenue Fund to the General Fund for the reimbursement of health and welfare program expenditures. $13,201,734 was transferred to the Sales Tax Revenue Fund from other governmental funds to provide additional resources for the health and welfare program expenditures. $9,668,533 was transferred from the General Fund to other governmental funds to pay debt service on health insurance financing bonds. An additional $5,628,134 was transferred from the General Fund to the Public Facility Corporation Debt Service Fund to provide resources for repayment of certificates of participation. $23,671,182 was transferred from the General Fund to other governmental funds and $5,789,767 was transferred between other governmental funds to supplement other fund sources for capital expenditures.
