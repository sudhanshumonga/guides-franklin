Chapter 7, Question 7.104.18

7.104.18.Q—Phase 1 and 2 governments are not required to retroactively report nonmajor infrastructure networks. If a phase 1 or 2 government has records to support some of its nonmajor infrastructure networks or has estimated the historical cost of some of its nonmajor infrastructure networks, can the government report those “retroactive” infrastructure assets?
A—Phase 1 and 2 governments can report nonmajor infrastructure networks subsequent to their initial transition if appropriate supporting documentation is available. For this purpose, a phase 1 or 2 government may be considered to be in transition subsequent to its required reporting of major general infrastructure. (See also Questions 7.85.5 and 7.104.17.)
