Chapter 6, Question 6.4.5

6.4.5.Q—In order to finance its own equipment purchase, a city's internal service fund issues a promissory note that is purchased by the city's general fund. Should the general fund report the note as an investment, or should this transaction be reported as an interfund loan?
A—The general and internal service funds should report the transaction as an interfund loan in the fund financial statements.
