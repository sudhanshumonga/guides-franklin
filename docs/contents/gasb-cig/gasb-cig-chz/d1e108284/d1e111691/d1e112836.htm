Chapter Z, Question Z.51.35

Z.51.35.Q—If a government was not required to retroactively report a computer software system at transition, should the government apply the provisions of Statement 51, as amended, to account for outlays associated with modifications of that computer software system performed subsequent to implementation?
A—Yes. As of the effective date of Statement 51, the provisions should be applied to modifications of computer software regardless of whether the original software being modified has been reported as an asset in the financial statements.
