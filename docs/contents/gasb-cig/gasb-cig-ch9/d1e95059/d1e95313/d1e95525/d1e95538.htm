Chapter 9, Question 9.8.1

9.8.1.Q—Are governments engaged only in business-type activities required to present information about changes in net position other than operating and nonoperating revenues and expenses?
A—Yes. The changes in net position information should lead to a total change in net position amount for each of the last 10 years, as required by paragraph 10 of Statement 44, as amended. Therefore, other changes in net position—such as capital contributions and additions to permanent endowments—should also be presented, not just revenues and expenses.
