Chapter Z, Question Z.42.2

Z.42.2.Q—Should an impairment loss be reported for capital assets accounted for using the modified approach that have incurred physical damage?
A—No. As a part of meeting the requirements to use the modified approach, management has made a commitment to maintain the network or subsystem at an established condition level. If a portion of the network or subsystem suffers physical damage, management is obligated to restore that portion to the established condition. Consequently, physical damage to capital assets accounted for using the modified approach is considered temporary in nature. (See Question 7.18.4.)
