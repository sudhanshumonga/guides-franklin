Chapter 7, Question 7.52.4

7.52.4.Q—How should IRC Section 457 plans that meet the criteria for reporting be presented?
A—IRC Section 457 plans, if reported, meet the definition of a pension (and other employee benefit) trust fund in paragraph 70 of Statement 34, as amended, and would be presented pursuant to the requirements in paragraphs 106–109 of Statement 34, as amended.
