Chapter 7, Question 7.16.7

7.16.7.Q—Is use of the modified approach linked to retroactive reporting of infrastructure? For example, can a phase 3 government that does not opt to retroactively report infrastructure account for its prospectively reported infrastructure using the modified approach?
A—The use of the modified approach is not limited to entities that are required to retroactively report infrastructure. A phase 3 government that opts to report infrastructure on a prospective basis may apply the modified approach if it meets the requirements outlined in paragraph 23 of Statement 34.
