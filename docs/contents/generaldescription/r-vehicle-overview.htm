Vehicle overview

Major system requirements support reuse of the vehicle and the two rockets. The nominal trip is 1 to 3 days in space. In returning to base the vehicle has a range of about 1000 nautical miles.

Thrust, directional, and reentry boosters, tank, and engines

The main vehicle has the following primary elements for liftoff, space travel, and reentry:
  • Two solid rocket boosters (SRB)
  • One external fuel tank (ET)
  • Three main onboard engines (OBE)

Payload

Payloads are carried in a bay 15 feet in diameter and 60 feet long.
