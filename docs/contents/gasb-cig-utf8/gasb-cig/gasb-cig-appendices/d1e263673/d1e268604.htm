Appendix B2-1: Statement 9, as Amended—Examples of Statements of Cash Flows
This nonauthoritative appendix provides illustrations of how Statement 9, as amended, might be applied for a variety of proprietary funds or business-type activities. The examples are designed to illustrate specific issues addressed in paragraph 6 of this Implementation Guide. Those questions may include references to particular examples that illustrate the issue addressed in the question. As a cross-reference, examples in this nonauthoritative appendix include appropriate footnote references to specific questions in paragraph 6.
Examples A–J are not as comprehensive as the illustration provided in example K. That example includes a discussion of relevant transactions and the other basic financial statements. Examples A–J include only the statement of cash flows and supporting schedules.
