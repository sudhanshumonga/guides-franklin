Chapter 10, Question 10.13.8

10.13.8Q—A government reports several derivative instruments. Some are reported as assets, others are liabilities. Can derivative instruments be reported net on the statement of net position—that is, assets minus liabilities?
A—Unless there is a right of offset, derivative instruments that are assets should be reported separately from those that are liabilities. This would be the case even if the derivative instruments are involved in a single hedging relationship.
