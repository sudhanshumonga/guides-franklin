Electrical Characteristics

All electrical parameters in all tables are specified under the following conditions, unless stated otherwise:
  • Typical values are based on TAMB=25 conkeyref: keys/degC and VDD= 3.3 V, by production test and/or technology characterization.
  • Radio performance numbers are measured in conducted mode, based on Silicon Laboratories reference designs using output power-specific external RF impedance-matching networks for interfacing to a 50 conkeyref: keys/ohm antenna.
  • Minimum and maximum values represent the worst conditions across supply voltage, process variation, and operating temperature, unless stated otherwise.
Refer to General Operating Conditions for more details about operational supply and temperature limits.
