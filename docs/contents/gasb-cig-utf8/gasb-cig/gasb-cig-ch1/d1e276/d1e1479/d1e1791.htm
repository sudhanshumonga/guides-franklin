Chapter 1, Question 1.9.12

1.9.12.Q—A government invests in a mutual fund that restricts its investments to obligations of the U.S. government or those explicitly guaranteed by the U.S. government. Is the credit quality rating of this mutual fund required to be disclosed?
A—Yes. Despite the holdings within the fund's portfolio, mutual funds are exposed to some degree of credit risk. Although certain U.S. government securities and agencies are guaranteed, mutual funds that invest in these securities are not.
