Chapter 8, Question 8.47.2

8.47.2.Q—Under what circumstances may an employer use the alternative measurement method?
A—Paragraph 11 of Statement 45, as amended, permits use of the alternative measurement method by a sole or agent employer whose single-employer or individual-employer OPEB plan has fewer than 100 total plan members (including employees in active service, terminated employees who have accumulated benefits but are not yet receiving them, and retirees and beneficiaries currently receiving benefits).
