Chapter 8, Question 8.43.2

8.43.2.Q—What additional information related to the required schedule of funding progress should be presented as notes to the schedule?
A—Employers should present as notes to the schedule of funding progress information about factors that significantly affect the interpretation of trends in the amounts reported. For example, an employer should disclose in notes to RSI changes in actuarial assumptions that have a significant effect on the amounts presented in the schedule of funding progress for one year compared to the information presented for prior years.
