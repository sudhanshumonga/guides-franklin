Chapter Z, Question Z.18.1

Z.18.1Q—A government operating a municipal solid waste landfill plans to construct a facility that will both collect landfill gas and generate electric power. Should this facility be accrued in the landfill liability under Statement 18 or should it be reported as a capital asset when constructed?
A—Paragraph 1 of Statement 18 notes that Statement applies to “closure and postclosure care costs that are required to be incurred by federal, state, or local laws or regulations.” A power generation facility is not a required part of closure and postclosure care. Therefore, the incremental outlays attributable to the power generation facility should be capitalized and not included in the landfill liability.
