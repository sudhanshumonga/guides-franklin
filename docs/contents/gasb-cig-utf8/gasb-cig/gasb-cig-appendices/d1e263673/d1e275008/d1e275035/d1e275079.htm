Example 2: A Pension System Accumulates Assets for Retiree Healthcare Benefits
Facts: A statewide pension system has a fund through which it accumulates assets contributed by the state and participating local governments for future retiree healthcare benefits.
Determination: This does not meet the definition of a public entity risk pool: Other postemployment benefits (OPEB) are being provided through this fund. OPEB is specifically excluded from the scope of Statement 10 in paragraph 2, as amended.
