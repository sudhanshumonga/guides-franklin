Chapter 1, Question 1.57.3

1.57.3.Q—When a debt investment carries a call option, is there a preferable way of disclosing the call option?
A—The majority of the interest rate risk disclosure methods outlined in paragraph 15 of Statement 40 can adequately communicate the effects of call options on debt investments. However, governments using the specific identification method of disclosing interest rate risk will need to separately disclose any call options existing on their debt investments. Call options may be identified in the narrative disclosure or may be footnoted to a schedule displaying the government's exposure to interest rate risk. (See Questions 1.60.1, 1.62.1, and 1.63.3.)
