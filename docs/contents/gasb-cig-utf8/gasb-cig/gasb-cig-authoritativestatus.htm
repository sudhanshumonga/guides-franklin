Authoritative Status of the Comprehensive Implementation Guide

GASB Statement No. 76, The Hierarchy of Generally Accepted Accounting Principles for State and Local Governments, identifies GASB Implementation Guides as a source of accounting and financial reporting guidance in Category B of the hierarchy of generally accepted accounting principles for state and local governments. Questions and answers and glossary definitions in GASB Implementation Guides are cleared for issuance by the GASB and are authoritative as Category B guidance.
This document incorporates questions and answers and glossary definitions from GASB Implementation Guides issued and effective though June 30, 2016. When presented in this document, questions and answers and glossary definitions from those Implementation Guides retain their authoritative status as Category B guidance. Illustrative materials and other appendices presented in this document are nonauthoritative.
The provisions of this Comprehensive Implementation Guide need not be applied to immaterial items.
