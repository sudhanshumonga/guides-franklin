Chapter 7, Question 7.72.6

7.72.6.Q—Do the additional display/disclosure requirements in paragraph 89 of Statement 34 (for significant transactions or other events that are either unusual or infrequent but are not within the control of management) for governmental funds also apply to proprietary funds?
A—The display/disclosure requirements in paragraph 89 of Statement 34 apply only to governmental fund reporting. Because proprietary funds are subject to the requirements of Statement 62, as amended, and paragraphs 100 and 101 of Statement 34, as amended, paragraph 89 does not refer to proprietary funds. (See Question 7.43.2 about unusual and infrequent.)
