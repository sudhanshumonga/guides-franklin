Chapter Z, Question Z.33.12

Z.33.12.Q—An entity levies amounts in its current property tax levy for future debt service payments. Should the amounts levied for the future-period debt service payments be reported as a deferred inflow of resources until those future periods start?
A—No. Governments often include amounts in their property tax levies that will be accumulated and not paid out until future periods (for example, amounts for other postemployment benefits). Unless a legal requirement specifies otherwise, the period for which these amounts are levied is the same as the period for which the rest of the taxes are levied.
