Chapter Z, Question Z.33.6

Z.33.6.Q— Paragraph 14 of Statement 33, as amended, requires recipients of resources with purpose restrictions to report the resulting net position (and fund balance, as appropriate) as restricted until the purpose restrictions are met. Can the recipient spend unrestricted resources for programs that are also supported by grants and continue to report the grant resources as restricted?
A—Yes, if a government's policy is to consider that unrestricted resources are used before restricted resources when an expense/expenditure is incurred for purposes for which both restricted and unrestricted amounts are available. (Paragraph 115h of Statement 34 requires governments to disclose that policy.)
