Chapter 7, Question 7.107.6

7.107.6.Q—If determining the actual historical costs of general infrastructure assets is not practical because of inadequate records, what alternatives are there to establish initial capitalizations from existing information?
A—Potential sources of estimates of historical costs include bond documents, capital outlay and capital projects fund expenditures in prior financial reports, engineering documents, capital budgets, and evidence of contract awards, such as those found in minutes of the governing body's meetings.
