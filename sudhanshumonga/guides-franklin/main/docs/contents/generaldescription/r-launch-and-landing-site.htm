Launch and landing site

Ensure the launch site meets minimum requirements.

Each launch site may have different configurations, but specific minimums must be met:

  • The launch site must meet physical dimension requirements:

    • at least 5 miles8km long;

    • at least 3 miles5km wide.

  • Site statements must be completed and approved for:

    • environmental, and;

    • safety.

  • Additional setup is specified in the companion document Site Setup.
