Chapter 7, Question 7.8.4

7.8.4.Q—If a government accounts for its risk-financing activities in its general fund (using the modified accrual basis of accounting), how should its claims liabilities and expenses be recognized in its government-wide financial statements?
A—The requirements for claims liability and expense recognition on the accrual basis are provided in Statement 10, paragraphs 53–5[6], as amended [, and paragraph 9 of Statement 30]. (See Question 3.54.1 and Illustration 1 in nonauthoritative Appendix B3-5.)
