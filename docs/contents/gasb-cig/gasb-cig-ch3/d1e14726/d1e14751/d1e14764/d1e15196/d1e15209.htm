Chapter 3, Question 3.7.1

3.7.1.Q—Are unemployment compensation claims that are provided under the joint state and federal unemployment insurance system within the scope of Statement 10, as amended?
A—Paragraph 3 excludes from the scope of Statement 10, as amended the recognition and measurement of liabilities for unemployment compensation claims. NCGA Statement 4, Accounting and Financial Reporting Principles for Claims and Judgments and Compensated Absences, as amended, and NCGA Interpretation 9, Certain Fund Classifications and Balance Sheet Accounts, as amended, provide guidance on those claims.
