Chapter 10, Question 10.46.9

10.46.9.Q—Should a government’s derivative instrument transactions be included in the investment policy disclosure required by paragraph 65 of Statement 3, as amended, paragraph 6 of Statement 40, and paragraph 76 of Statement 53?
A—To the extent a government’s participation in derivative instrument transactions represents investments, the government’s policy for entering into those transactions should be included in the investment policy disclosure required by paragraph 65 of Statement 3, as amended, paragraph 6 of Statement 40, and paragraph 76 of Statement 53.
