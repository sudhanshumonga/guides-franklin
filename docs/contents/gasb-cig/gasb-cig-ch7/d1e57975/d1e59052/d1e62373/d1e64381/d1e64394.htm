Chapter 7, Question 7.25.1

7.25.1.Q—Which component of net position should include an equity interest in a joint venture?
A—An equity interest in a joint venture that is reported as an asset in the government-wide statement of net position (or as an asset of an enterprise fund) should be included in the computation of the unrestricted component of net position. An equity interest in a joint venture is generally not restricted, as defined in paragraph 34, of Statement 34, as amended, nor is it a capital asset, even though it may represent equity primarily in capital assets of the joint venture. (See Question 7.62.3 about governmental fund reporting.)
