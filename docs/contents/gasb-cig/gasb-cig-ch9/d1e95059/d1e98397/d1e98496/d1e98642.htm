Chapter 9, Question 9.37.6

9.37.6.Q—Are public colleges and universities required to present the operating indicators such as freshman admissions and degrees earned?
A—Statement 44 does not specify the particular operating indicators that public colleges and universities or any other type of governmental entity should present. However, the information shown in Exhibit F-5 in nonauthoritative Appendix C of Statement 44 and Exhibits E-11 and E-12 in nonauthoritative Appendix B9-2 commonly has been presented by public colleges and universities for many years and is valued by financial report users. Colleges and universities should use professional judgment to select the indicators that best help them to achieve the objectives of operating information and should clearly note any methods or assumptions used to derive the information.
