Launch pad escape

If there is a problem on the launch pad you can escape through a slidewire basket. There are multiple exits to many exit locations depending on the problem.

There are unique slidewire escapes for each member of the crew and all passengers. The escape landing zone is over 1000 feet from the launch pad and leads to a secured bunker.

The escape bunker contains:

  • breathing air

  • fire equipment

  • first aid equipment

  • communications equipment
