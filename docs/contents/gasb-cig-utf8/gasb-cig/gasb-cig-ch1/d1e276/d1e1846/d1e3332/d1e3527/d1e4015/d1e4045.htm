Chapter 1, Question 1.33.2

1.33.2.Q—Are deposits that are covered by irrevocable standby letters of credit issued by the Federal Home Loan Bank exposed to custodial credit risk?
A—No. An irrevocable letter of credit can be considered a form of insurance if the bank or the bank's affiliate did not issue it and it provides a scope of coverage substantially the same as that provided by federal deposit insurance.
