Chapter 4, Question 4.49.2

4.49.2.Q—Does a joint venture's reliance on continued funding have to be reliance on funding in the form of cash contributions, or can it be reliance on other forms of support from the participating government?
A—The form of the funding would not matter; the dependence of the joint venture's continued existence on the funding, whether in the form of cash subsidies, purchases of the joint venture's goods or services, or some other form, would be the relevant factor.
