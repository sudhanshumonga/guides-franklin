Braking

Each of the landing gear wheels is equipped with ABS disc brakes and a mix of hardware and software to prevent skidding.
When approaching the breaks are used to slow the vehicle and, if less than 5000 feet of runway remain and groundspeed is over 140 knots, then maximum breaking is applied. This brings the vehicle to a full stop at which point the message “wheels stop” is reported. Due to the high speed at landing ABS is used to allow the wheels to maintain traction with the tarmac and avoid locking up which results in an uncontrolled skid.
