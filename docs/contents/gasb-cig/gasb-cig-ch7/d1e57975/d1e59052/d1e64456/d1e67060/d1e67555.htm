Chapter 7, Question 7.47.16

7.47.16.Q—To what extent should interfund transfers be eliminated for the statement of activities?
A—Interfund transfers, as defined in paragraph 112b(1) of Statement 34, within governmental activities and within business-type activities should be eliminated for the statement of activities. Only the net amount transferred between governmental and business-type activities should be reported. The internal "eliminations" can be achieved and the "net" amount easily determined by combining transfers in and transfers out within each category as a single "transfers" amount for the statement of activities.
