Chapter 7, Question 7.70.3

7.70.3.Q—How should a government report a change in the allowance for uncollectible accounts that relates to loans receivable and is therefore not related to a revenue account?
A—The change in an uncollectible loan allowance account should be reported as an expense—there is no related revenue account to reduce. A forgiven or uncollectible loan becomes, in substance, a gift, grant, or contribution, and is properly reported as an expense.
