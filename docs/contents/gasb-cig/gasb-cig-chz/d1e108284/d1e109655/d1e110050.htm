Chapter Z, Question Z.42.11

Z.42.11.Q—Does the guidance on insurance recoveries in paragraphs 21 and 22 of Statement 42 apply to situations in which an insurance policy other than the government's insurance policy covers the loss? For example, if a freight company's truck hits and damages a government's bridge, would recovery of the loss through the truck owner's insurance policy be accounted for in accordance with paragraphs 21 and 22?
A—Yes, the guidance in paragraphs 21 and 22 applies to any loss covered by insurance, whether or not the policyholder is the government.
