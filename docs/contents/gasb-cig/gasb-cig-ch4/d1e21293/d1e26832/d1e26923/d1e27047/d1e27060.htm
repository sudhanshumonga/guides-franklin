Chapter 4, Question 4.49.1

4.49.1.Q—What is ongoing financial responsibility? (Q&A14-132)
A—Paragraph 71 of Statement 14 states that a participating government has an ongoing financial responsibility for a joint venture if it is obligated in some manner for the debts of the joint venture (see Question 4.22.4), or if the joint venture's continued existence depends on continued funding by the government. For example, if a mass transit authority is dependent on appropriations from a city to subsidize recurring operating deficits caused by insufficient user fee revenues, the city would have an ongoing financial responsibility.
