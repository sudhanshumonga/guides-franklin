Chapter 1, Question 1.5.3

1.5.3.Q—A state government issues a bond that contains specific covenants related to the investment of the proceeds. Should this be considered an investment policy?
A—Yes. The disclosure of relevant deposit or investment policies communicates to the readers of the financial statements a government's tolerance for risk. The disclosure of such policies, when identified risks warrant such a disclosure, serves to indicate the risk tolerance information that Statement 40 was designed to convey.
