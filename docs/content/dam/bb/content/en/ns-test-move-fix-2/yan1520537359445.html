View organization details

  1. On the menu bar, click Org Connect.
  2. Do one of the following:
    • Click All organizations.
    • Click My network.
  3. In the list of organizations, click the name of the organization that you want to view. On the My network page, you can expand the row for the organization to view a summary of the details.
    The organization details are displayed. You can view the organization name, sector, web address, organization owner, phone number, street address, and the connections that you have set up with the organization.
