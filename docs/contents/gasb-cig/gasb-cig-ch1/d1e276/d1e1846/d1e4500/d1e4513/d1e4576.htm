Chapter 1, Question 1.43.3

1.43.3.Q—Is a government exposed to custodial credit risk on the portion of the reported amount of any repurchase agreement that exceeds the fair value of the underlying securities?
A—Yes. Custodial credit risk includes the portion of the reported amount of any repurchase agreement that exceeds the fair value of the underlying securities. The disclosure of the repurchase agreement's custodial credit risk is consistent with Statement 40 . . . .
