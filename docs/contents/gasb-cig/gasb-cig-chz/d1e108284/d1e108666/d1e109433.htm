Chapter Z, Question Z.33.21

Z.33.21.Q— Paragraph 24 of Statement 33 states that if a governmental provider does not specify a time requirement, the applicable period for recognition is the provider's fiscal year. What is the applicable period if the provider is not a government?
A—Paragraph 78 of the Basis for Conclusions of Statement 33 states that "when a nongovernmental provider does not specify otherwise, [the] Statement requires recipients to recognize the awards in full in the first period that use is permitted and all other applicable eligibility requirements have been met [subject to availability in governmental funds]."
