Chapter 7, Question 7.12.16

7.12.16.Q—Should land or easements associated with infrastructure assets, such as right-of-way easements for highways, be classified as infrastructure?
A—No. Land or easements associated with infrastructure should be classified as “land”—or another appropriate category, such as “right-of-way easements”—not as “infrastructure” and should be reported initially at acquisition value. The appropriate depreciation (or amortization) provisions also should be applied, for example, land generally should not be depreciated because it is inexhaustible.
