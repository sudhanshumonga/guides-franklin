Chapter 7, Question 7.91.13

7.91.13.Q—Paragraph 130 of Statement 34, as amended, states that a budgetary comparison is required for "the general fund and for each major special revenue fund that has a legally adopted annual budget." Does the legally adopted requirement apply to the general fund or only to the major special revenue funds?
A—The legally adopted requirement applies to the general fund as well as the major special revenue funds. For example, if a special-purpose government maintains a general fund (or its equivalent) but is not legally required to adopt or does not legally adopt a budget, a budgetary comparison schedule should not be presented in the basic statements or as RSI. (See also Questions 7.91.5 and 7.91.6.)
