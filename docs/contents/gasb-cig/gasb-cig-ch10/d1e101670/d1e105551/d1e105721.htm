Chapter 10, Question 10.46.7

10.46.7.Q—Should fully benefit-responsive synthetic guaranteed investment contracts (SGICs) be included in the summary derivative instrument table that shows the changes in fair value as required by paragraph 69 of Statement 53?
A—No. The table is limited to derivative instruments measured at fair value. Because fully benefit-responsive SGICs are not measured at fair value, they should not be included in the summary derivative instrument table.
