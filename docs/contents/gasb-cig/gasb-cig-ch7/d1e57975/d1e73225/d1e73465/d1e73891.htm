Chapter 7, Question 7.104.16

7.104.16.Q—Although phase 3 governments are not required to retroactively report infrastructure, can they choose to retroactively report infrastructure in a future year? Is there a cutoff, such as the additional four years that phase 1 and 2 governments are accorded to retroactively report infrastructure, for retroactive reporting for phase 3 governments?
A—Phase 3 governments may choose at any time to retroactively report infrastructure. Paragraph 148 of Statement 34 encourages them to retroactively report infrastructure and does not place a time restriction on when this should occur. (See Question 7.104.15.)
