Chapter 9, Question 9.11.2

9.11.2.Q—Are governments required to present a separate schedule of tax revenues by source?
A—No. Paragraph 12 of Statement 44 states that governments should, at a minimum, present changes in fund balance information at the level required by Statement 34 for the governmental funds statement of revenues, expenditures, and changes in fund balances. However, paragraph 12 also states that governments may provide additional detail on the face of the schedule or in an accompanying schedule.
