Chapter 8, Question 8.12.1

8.12.1.Q—A town provides disability and lump sum death benefits for former volunteers of the town's fire and rescue department who meet certain age and length-of-service criteria. Does Statement 45 apply even though the benefit recipients are not "employees" of the government?
A—Yes. For financial accounting and reporting purposes, the benefits provided by the town to former volunteers would be considered compensation for services received by the town during the volunteers' active service periods, and, therefore, the requirements of Statement 45 would apply.
