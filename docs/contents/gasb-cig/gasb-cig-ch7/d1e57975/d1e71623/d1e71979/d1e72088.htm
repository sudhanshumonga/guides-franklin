Chapter 7, Question 7.86.4

7.86.4.Q—To meet the requirement for segment reporting, is it necessary for the debt to be secured solely by the pledged revenue stream, or can it also be backed by the full faith and credit of the issuing government?
A—The definition of a segment in paragraph 122 of Statement 34, as amended, includes a requirement that there is a specific identifiable revenue stream pledged in support of revenue bonds or other revenue-backed debt. It is not a requirement that the debt be backed solely by the pledged revenues. The additional "full faith and credit" support does not negate the requirement to make the segment disclosures if all the criteria in paragraph 122, as amended, are met.
