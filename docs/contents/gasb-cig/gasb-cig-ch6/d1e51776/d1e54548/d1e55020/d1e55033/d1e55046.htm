Chapter 6, Question 6.42.1

6.42.1.Q—Paragraph 17 of Statement 31 provides that all applicable GASB pronouncements should be applied in the separate or stand-alone financial reports of governmental external investment pools. What are the other GASB pronouncements that most likely apply?
A—The other GASB pronouncements most likely to apply to governmental external investment pools are Statement 3, as amended; Statement 28, as amended; Statement 34, as amended; Statement No. 37, Basic Financial Statements—and Management’s Discussion and Analysis—for State and Local Governments: Omnibus, as amended; Statement 38, as amended; Statement 40, as amended; Statement 53, as amended; Statement 62, as amended; Statement 72; Statement 79; and Interpretation 3, as amended. However, there might be other transactions or balances subject to GASB pronouncements. All GASB pronouncements should be reviewed for applicability.
