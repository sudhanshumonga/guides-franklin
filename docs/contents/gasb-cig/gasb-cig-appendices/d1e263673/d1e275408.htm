Appendix B3-3: Statement 10, as Amended—Calculation of Pool Premium Deficiencies
Paragraphs 34–36 of Statement 10, as amended, require premium deficiencies to be calculated and reported for pools. Those paragraphs provide details on the types of costs that are required to be considered in this nonauthoritative calculation.
