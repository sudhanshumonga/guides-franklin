Chapter 6, Question 6.13.2

6.13.2.Q—If investments are reported at fair value, can accounting records be kept on the cost (or amortized cost) basis, with financial statement adjustments for fair value?
A—Yes. There is no requirement to journalize fair value adjustments or to keep accounting records based on fair value. Cost-based records are acceptable, provided that adjustments to fair value are made when financial statements are prepared.
