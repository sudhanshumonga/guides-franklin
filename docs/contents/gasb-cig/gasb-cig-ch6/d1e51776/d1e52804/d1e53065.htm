Chapter 6, Question 6.12.11

6.12.11.Q—How should the reporting of investments at fair value be applied to zero-coupon bonds?
A—The bonds should be reported at their fair value at the reporting date, and any change in fair value should be reported as a change in net position. The fair value of the bonds ultimately will approach face value—the amount of the proceeds that will be received—as the maturity date nears.
