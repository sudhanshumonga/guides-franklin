Chapter 7, Question 7.47.23

7.47.23.Q—How should transactions between a university and its auxiliary enterprises be reported? Should they be eliminated for the statement of revenues, expenses, and changes in fund net position?
A—If the university reports its activities as both governmental and business-type, internal activity should be reported in accordance with the provisions of paragraph 112 of Statement 34, as amended, and eliminations made as required by paragraphs 59 and 60 of that Statement. However, if the university is reported as a single-column BTA, internal transactions between the university and its auxiliary enterprises should be eliminated in the consolidation process.
