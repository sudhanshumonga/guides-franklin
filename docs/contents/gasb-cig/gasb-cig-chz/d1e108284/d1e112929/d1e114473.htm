Chapter Z, Question Z.54.47

Z.54.47.Q—Does paragraph 24 of Statement 54 require disclosure of specific individual encumbrances by fund balance classification, if significant?
A—No. The requirement in paragraph 24 is to disclose the total amount of encumbrances by major fund and nonmajor funds in the aggregate, if significant. Disclosure of individual encumbrances is not required and classification of encumbered amounts as restricted, committed, or assigned also is not required.
